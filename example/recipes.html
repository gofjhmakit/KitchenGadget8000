🐟 Herb Roasted Salmon

Serves 4 · 25 min

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 400 g asparagus
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Place salmon and asparagus on a tray.
  2. Brush with herb oil mixture.
  3. Roast at 200°C for 14 minutes.
