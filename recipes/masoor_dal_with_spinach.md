## Ingredients
- 250 g red lentils (masoor dal)
- 200 g baby spinach
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tbsp oil
- 800 ml water
- Salt
- Lemon wedges

## Instructions
1. Simmer rinsed masoor dal with turmeric and water until creamy, about 20 minutes.
2. Heat oil; toast cumin seeds.
3. Sauté onion until soft; add tomatoes and coriander powder.
4. Fold in spinach until wilted.
5. Combine with dal, season, and serve with lemon.

## Notes
Protein-rich red lentil dal with greens.
