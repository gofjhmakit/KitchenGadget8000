## Ingredients
- 300 g poached chicken breast, shredded
- 2 cucumbers, julienned
- 2 tbsp black vinegar
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp chili oil
- Sliced scallions
- Cilantro
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Shred warm poached chicken.
2. Salt cucumbers; drain.
3. Whisk vinegar, soy, and chili oil.
4. Toss chicken and cucumber with dressing.
5. Garnish with scallions, cilantro, and sesame.

## Notes
Bang bang ji-inspired salad.
