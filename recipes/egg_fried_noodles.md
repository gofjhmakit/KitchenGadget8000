## Ingredients
- 250 g egg noodles
- 2 eggs
- 150 g mixed vegetables
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 2 spring onions
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Cook noodles; drain.
2. Scramble eggs in a hot wok; set aside.
3. Stir-fry vegetables, then add noodles and soy sauce.
4. Return eggs; finish with sesame oil and spring onions.

## Notes
Use leftover noodles if available.
