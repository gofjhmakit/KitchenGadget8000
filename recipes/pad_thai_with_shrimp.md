## Ingredients
- 200 g rice noodles
- 200 g shrimp, peeled
- 2 eggs
- 100 g bean sprouts
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp tamarind paste
- 2 tbsp palm sugar
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp peanut oil
- Crushed peanuts
- Lime wedges

## Instructions
1. Soak rice noodles in warm water 20 minutes; drain.
2. Stir-fry garlic and shrimp in peanut oil until pink.
3. Push aside; scramble eggs in the wok.
4. Add noodles, fish sauce, tamarind, and palm sugar; toss 3 minutes.
5. Fold in bean sprouts; serve with peanuts and lime.

## Notes
Classic sweet-sour Thai stir-fried noodles.
