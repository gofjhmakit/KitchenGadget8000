## Ingredients
- 1 whole chicken
- 200 g rice noodles
- 1 onion, charred
- 3 cm ginger, charred
- 2 star anise
- Fish sauce
- Fresh cilantro and Thai basil
- Lime

## Instructions
1. Simmer chicken with charred onion, ginger, and star anise 90 minutes.
2. Shred chicken; strain broth.
3. Season broth with fish sauce.
4. Blanch noodles; assemble bowls.
5. Top with chicken, herbs, and lime.

## Notes
Light chicken pho broth.
