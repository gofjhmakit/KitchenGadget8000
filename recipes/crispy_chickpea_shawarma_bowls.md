## Ingredients
- 400 g chickpeas
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp paprika
- 250 g cooked rice
- 150 g cucumber yogurt

## Instructions
1. Roast chickpeas with oil and spices until crisp.
2. Serve over rice with cucumber yogurt and salad.
3. Finish with lemon and herbs.

## Notes
A fast pantry dinner.
