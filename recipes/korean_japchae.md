## Ingredients
- 200 g sweet potato glass noodles
- 150 g spinach
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 100 g shiitake mushrooms
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 garlic cloves
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Boil glass noodles 6 minutes; drain and cut.
2. Blanch spinach; squeeze dry.
3. Stir-fry carrot and mushrooms until tender.
4. Toss noodles with soy sauce, sugar, garlic, and sesame oil.
5. Combine vegetables and noodles; garnish with sesame seeds.

## Notes
Celebratory Korean sweet potato noodle dish.
