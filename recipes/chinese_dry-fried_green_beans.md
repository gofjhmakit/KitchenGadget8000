## Ingredients
- 400 g long green beans, trimmed
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp black vinegar
- 2 dried chilies
- 3 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tsp sugar

## Instructions
1. Blister green beans in hot oil until wrinkled.
2. Add garlic and chilies; stir-fry 1 minute.
3. Add soy sauce, vinegar, and sugar.
4. Toss until glazed.
5. Serve hot.

## Notes
Sichuan gan bian si ji dou.
