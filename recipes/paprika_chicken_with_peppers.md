## Ingredients
- 600 g chicken breast
- 2 peppers, sliced
- 1 onion
- 2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 150 ml chicken stock
- 80 ml cream

## Instructions
1. Brown chicken pieces in olive oil.
2. Add onion, peppers, and paprika.
3. Pour in stock and simmer until chicken is cooked.
4. Stir in cream and season.

## Notes
Serve with rice or barley.
