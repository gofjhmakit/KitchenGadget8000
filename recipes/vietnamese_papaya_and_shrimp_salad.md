## Ingredients
- 200 g green papaya, shredded
- 150 g poached shrimp
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Fresh herbs
- Crushed peanuts
- Crispy shallots

## Instructions
1. Shred papaya into thin strips.
2. Whisk fish sauce, lime, and sugar.
3. Toss papaya with shrimp and herbs.
4. Dress and plate.
5. Top with peanuts and fried shallots.

## Notes
Light Vietnamese shrimp salad.
