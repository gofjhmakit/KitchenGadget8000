## Ingredients
- 1 cup milk
- 1/4 tsp ground cardamom
- Pinch of saffron threads
- 2 cups frozen mango chunks
- 1 cup greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp honey

## Instructions
1. Add frozen mango chunks, Greek yogurt, milk, honey, and cardamom to a blender.
2. Blend on high until smooth and frothy, about 60 seconds.
3. Taste and adjust sweetness -- ripe mangoes may need less honey.
4. Pour into glasses. If feeling traditional, sprinkle a tiny pinch of cardamom on top.
5. Close your eyes, take a sip, and pretend you're somewhere tropical.
