## Ingredients
- 600 g cod fillets
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 garlic cloves
- 400 g chopped tomatoes
- 60 g olives
- 1 tsp capers

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion and garlic in oil.
2. Add tomatoes, olives, capers, and simmer 10 minutes.
3. Nestle cod into the sauce and cook gently until flaky.
4. Finish with parsley.

## Notes
Serve over soft polenta.
