## Ingredients
- 400 g carrots
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 240 g chickpeas
- 2 tbsp tahini
- 1 lemon
- 1 garlic clove

## Instructions
1. Roast carrots until soft and lightly caramelized.
2. Blend with chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and salt.
3. Loosen with water until creamy.
4. Serve with crudités or crackers.

## Notes
Sweet and earthy.
