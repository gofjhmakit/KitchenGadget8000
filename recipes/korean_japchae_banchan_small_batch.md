## Ingredients
- 100 g sweet potato glass noodles
- 100 g spinach
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp sugar
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Boil noodles; cut into short lengths.
2. Blanch spinach; squeeze dry.
3. Stir-fry carrot briefly.
4. Toss everything with soy, sesame oil, and sugar.
5. Garnish with sesame seeds.

## Notes
Mini portion japchae side dish.
