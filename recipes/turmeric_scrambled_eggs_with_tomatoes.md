## Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- 1 tbsp milk
- 1 tsp butter
- 1 pinch turmeric
- 150 g cherry tomatoes
- 1 tbsp parsley

## Instructions
1. Whisk eggs with milk, turmeric, salt, and pepper.
2. Burst tomatoes in butter over medium heat.
3. Add eggs and stir gently until softly set.
4. Finish with parsley.

## Notes
Serve with rye toast.
