## Ingredients
- 400 g white cabbage, shredded
- 1 apple, julienned
- 1 carrot, grated
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp honey
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Combine cabbage, apple, and carrot.
2. Whisk vinegar, oil, honey, and salt.
3. Toss and rest 10 minutes before serving.

## Notes
Crunchy budget slaw.
