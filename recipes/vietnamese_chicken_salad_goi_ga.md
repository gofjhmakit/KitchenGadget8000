## Ingredients
- 300 g poached chicken breast, shredded
- 200 g cabbage, shredded
- 1 carrot, julienned
- Fresh mint and cilantro
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Sliced shallots
- Crushed peanuts

## Instructions
1. Shred poached chicken while warm.
2. Combine cabbage, carrot, and herbs.
3. Whisk fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar.
4. Toss chicken and vegetables with dressing.
5. Top with shallots and peanuts.

## Notes
Fresh herb Vietnamese chicken salad.
