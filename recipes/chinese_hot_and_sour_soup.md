## Ingredients
- 1 L chicken broth
- 100 g tofu, julienned
- 50 g wood ear mushrooms
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 3 tbsp black vinegar
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- White pepper
- Cornstarch slurry
- Sliced scallions

## Instructions
1. Simmer broth with mushrooms and tofu.
2. Season with vinegar, soy, and white pepper.
3. Thicken lightly with cornstarch slurry.
4. Drizzle beaten eggs in thin stream.
5. Garnish with scallions.

## Notes
Sichuan restaurant-style soup.
