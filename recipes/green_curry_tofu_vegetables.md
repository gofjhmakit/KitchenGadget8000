## Ingredients
- 400 g firm tofu
- 1 tbsp oil
- 2 tbsp green curry paste
- 400 ml coconut milk
- 1 broccoli
- 1 red pepper
- 150 g sugar snap peas

## Instructions
1. Brown tofu cubes in oil.
2. Add curry paste and cook 1 minute.
3. Stir in coconut milk and vegetables.
4. Simmer until vegetables are crisp-tender and serve with rice.

## Notes
A squeeze of lime wakes it up.
