## Ingredients
- 100 g each toor and moong dal
- 2 drumsticks, cut into pieces
- 1 tomato, chopped
- 2 tbsp sambar powder
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 8 curry leaves
- Tamarind paste (1 tsp)
- 2 tbsp oil
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Cook dals and drumsticks with water and salt until tender.
2. Add sambar powder and tamarind; simmer 10 minutes.
3. Temper mustard seeds and curry leaves in oil.
4. Pour over dal.
5. Serve with steamed rice.

## Notes
South Indian dal with moringa pods.
