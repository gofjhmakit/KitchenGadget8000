## Ingredients
- 500 g beef shank
- 400 g hand-pulled or fresh wheat noodles
- 1 daikon, sliced
- 2 star anise
- Sichuan peppercorns
- Chili oil
- Cilantro
- White radish

## Instructions
1. Simmer beef shank with spices and daikon 2 hours until tender.
2. Slice beef thin.
3. Boil noodles separately.
4. Ladle clear spiced broth over noodles.
5. Top with beef, cilantro, and chili oil.

## Notes
Clear Chinese beef noodle soup.
