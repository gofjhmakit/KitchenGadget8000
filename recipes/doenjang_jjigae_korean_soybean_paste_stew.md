## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp doenjang
- 500 ml water
- 150 g zucchini, cubed
- 100 g potatoes, cubed
- 200 g firm tofu
- 1/2 onion
- 2 cloves garlic
- Green chili
- Scallions

## Instructions
1. Dissolve doenjang in water in pot.
2. Add potato, onion, and garlic; simmer 10 minutes.
3. Add zucchini and tofu.
4. Simmer 8 minutes.
5. Finish with chili and scallions.

## Notes
Hearty Korean soybean stew.
