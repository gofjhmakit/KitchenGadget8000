## Ingredients
- 200 g yellow egg noodles
- 200 g rice noodles
- 200 g pork belly, sliced
- 200 g shrimp
- 3 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 500 ml pork stock
- 2 garlic cloves
- Sliced cabbage
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Stir-fry pork and garlic until browned.
2. Add both noodles and dark soy; toss 2 minutes.
3. Pour in stock; simmer until absorbed and caramelized.
4. Add shrimp and cabbage; cook 3 minutes.
5. Serve with lime and sambal.

## Notes
Dark soy braised mixed noodles.
