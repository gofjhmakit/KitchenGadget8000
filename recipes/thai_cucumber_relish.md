## Ingredients
- 2 cucumbers, diced
- 2 tbsp vinegar
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp chopped cilantro
- 1 shallot, minced
- Bird's eye chili, minced

## Instructions
1. Dice cucumbers finely.
2. Dissolve sugar in vinegar.
3. Toss with shallot and chili.
4. Fold in cilantro.
5. Serve with satay or curries.

## Notes
Refreshing Thai ajad relish.
