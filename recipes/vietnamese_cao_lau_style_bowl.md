## Ingredients
- 200 g thick rice noodles
- 200 g pork shoulder, sliced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp five-spice powder
- 100 g bean sprouts
- Fresh mint
- Rice crackers
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Marinate pork with soy sauce and five-spice 30 minutes.
2. Grill pork until charred; slice.
3. Blanch thick rice noodles.
4. Layer noodles with pork, sprouts, and mint.
5. Crush rice crackers on top.

## Notes
Hoi An-inspired noodle bowl.
