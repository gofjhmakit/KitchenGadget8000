## Ingredients
- 500 g chicken breast
- 1.2 L chicken broth
- 150 g orzo
- 2 eggs
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Poach chicken in broth until cooked; shred and return to pot.
2. Cook orzo in the broth until tender.
3. Whisk eggs with lemon juice.
4. Temper eggs with hot broth, then stir back into soup off heat.
5. Season and serve.

## Notes
Light avgolemono-style soup.
