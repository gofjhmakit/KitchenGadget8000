time: 20 min
servings: 4
tags: [healthy, fiber, vegetarian]

---

## Ingredients
- 300 g broccoli, finely chopped
- 2 carrots, grated
- 60 g red cabbage, shredded
- 40 g pumpkin seeds
- 30 g raisins
- 2 tbsp tahini
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp maple syrup

## Instructions
1. Steam broccoli 2 minutes then cool.
2. Whisk tahini, lemon juice, maple syrup, and 2 tbsp water.
3. Mix broccoli, carrot, cabbage, seeds, and raisins.
4. Coat with dressing and serve.

## Notes
Keeps its crunch for lunchboxes.
