## Ingredients
- 200 g wide rice noodles
- 200 g chicken thigh, sliced
- 100 g Chinese broccoli
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 2 eggs
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Soak wide rice noodles until soft.
2. Stir-fry garlic and chicken until cooked.
3. Push aside; scramble eggs in wok.
4. Add noodles, oyster sauce, and dark soy; char over high heat.
5. Toss in Chinese broccoli briefly.

## Notes
Sweet soy wide noodle classic.
