## Ingredients
- 300 g cooked rice
- 150 g spinach
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 100 g bean sprouts
- 150 g beef, sliced
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp gochujang
- Sesame oil
- Soy sauce

## Instructions
1. Blanch and season each vegetable separately.
2. Stir-fry beef with soy sauce.
3. Fry egg sunny-side up.
4. Arrange rice with vegetables and beef.
5. Serve with gochujang and sesame oil.

## Notes
Colorful mixed Korean rice bowl.
