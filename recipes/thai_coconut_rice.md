## Ingredients
- 300 g jasmine rice
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 200 ml water
- 1 tsp salt
- Pandan leaf (optional)

## Instructions
1. Rinse rice until water runs clear.
2. Combine rice, coconut milk, water, salt, and pandan.
3. Simmer covered on low 18 minutes.
4. Rest 10 minutes off heat.
5. Fluff and serve.

## Notes
Fragrant Thai coconut rice.
