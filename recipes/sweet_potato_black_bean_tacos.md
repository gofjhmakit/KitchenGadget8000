## Ingredients
- 500 g sweet potato, cubed
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 400 g black beans
- 8 corn tortillas
- 1 avocado
- lime wedges

## Instructions
1. Roast sweet potato with oil and cumin at 220°C until soft.
2. Warm black beans with a pinch of salt.
3. Fill tortillas with sweet potato, beans, and avocado.
4. Serve with lime.

## Notes
Great with shredded cabbage.
