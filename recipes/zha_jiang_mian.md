## Ingredients
- 400 g fresh wheat noodles
- 300 g ground pork
- 3 tbsp sweet bean paste
- 1 cucumber, julienned
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Cook noodles; drain and rinse.
2. Brown pork with garlic in oil.
3. Add sweet bean paste, soy sauce, wine, and sugar; simmer 10 minutes.
4. Spoon meat sauce over noodles.
5. Top with julienned cucumber.

## Notes
Beijing noodles with fermented bean sauce.
