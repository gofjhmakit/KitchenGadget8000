## Ingredients
- 250 g red lentils
- 1/2 onion, sliced
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 inch cinnamon stick
- 8 curry leaves
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 400 ml coconut milk
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Rinse lentils and simmer in water until soft.
2. Blend half the cooked lentils smooth.
3. Sauté onion, garlic, cinnamon, and curry leaves in coconut oil.
4. Add curry powder, blended lentils, whole lentils, and coconut milk.
5. Simmer 10 minutes until creamy.

## Notes
Coconut red lentil staple of Sri Lanka.
