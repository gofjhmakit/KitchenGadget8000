## Ingredients
- 2 ripe avocados, cubed
- 150 g imitation crab or real crab
- 2 tbsp Japanese mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Cucumber, diced
- Tobiko or sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Cube avocado and toss with lemon juice.
2. Shred crab and dice cucumber.
3. Fold gently with Japanese mayo.
4. Chill 10 minutes.
5. Serve topped with tobiko or sesame seeds.

## Notes
Light Japanese seafood salad.
