## Ingredients
- 600 g chicken breast
- 1 l chicken stock
- 20 g ginger
- 4 spring onions
- 2 tsp sesame oil
- 250 g cooked jasmine rice

## Instructions
1. Poach chicken gently in stock with sliced ginger until cooked through.
2. Shred chicken and toss with spring onion and sesame oil.
3. Reduce a little of the broth for spooning over.
4. Serve over warm rice.

## Notes
Add steamed greens on the side.
