## Ingredients
- 400 g soft tofu, cubed
- 150 g ground pork
- 1 tbsp doubanjiang
- 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorn powder
- 2 garlic cloves
- 500 ml chicken broth
- Cornstarch slurry
- Scallions

## Instructions
1. Brown pork with doubanjiang and garlic.
2. Add broth and gently simmer tofu 5 minutes.
3. Thicken with cornstarch slurry.
4. Dust with Sichuan pepper powder.
5. Garnish with scallions.

## Notes
Silky spicy Sichuan tofu.
