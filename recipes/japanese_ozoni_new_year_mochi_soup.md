## Ingredients
- 4 pieces kirimochi (rice cakes)
- 800 ml dashi
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 150 g chicken, sliced
- Carrot and daikon slices
- Spinach
- Yuzu zest

## Instructions
1. Simmer dashi with soy and mirin.
2. Poach chicken and root vegetables.
3. Grill or toast mochi until puffed.
4. Place mochi in bowls.
5. Ladle soup over with vegetables and yuzu zest.

## Notes
Celebratory clear mochi soup.
