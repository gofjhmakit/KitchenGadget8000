## Ingredients
- 250 g toor dal
- 300 g spinach, chopped
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 green chilies, slit
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tbsp oil
- Fresh lemon juice
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Simmer toor dal with turmeric until soft.
2. Sauté cumin, onion, and green chilies in oil.
3. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
4. Stir spinach mixture into dal and simmer 8 minutes.
5. Finish with lemon juice and salt.

## Notes
Classic spinach dal from North India.
