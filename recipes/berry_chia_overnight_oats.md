## Ingredients
- 100 g rolled oats
- 20 g chia seeds
- 250 ml milk of choice
- 120 g mixed berries
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 80 g Greek yogurt

## Instructions
1. Mix oats, chia, milk, and maple syrup in a jar.
2. Refrigerate overnight.
3. Top with yogurt and berries before serving.

## Notes
Add toasted nuts for crunch.
