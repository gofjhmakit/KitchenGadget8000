## Ingredients
- 200 g rice noodles
- 250 g chicken thigh, minced
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- Thai basil leaves
- 3 garlic cloves
- 2 bird's eye chilies
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Soak rice noodles; drain.
2. Stir-fry garlic and chilies.
3. Add minced chicken; cook through.
4. Toss noodles with fish sauce and oyster sauce.
5. Finish with Thai basil off heat.

## Notes
Pad kra pao meets noodles.
