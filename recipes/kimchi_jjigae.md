## Ingredients
- 2 cups aged kimchi, chopped
- 200 g firm tofu, cubed
- 150 g pork belly, sliced
- 1 tbsp gochugaru
- 500 ml water or anchovy broth
- Scallions
- Garlic

## Instructions
1. Sauté pork and kimchi 5 minutes.
2. Add broth and gochugaru; simmer 15 minutes.
3. Add tofu cubes.
4. Simmer 5 more minutes.
5. Garnish with scallions.

## Notes
Comfort spicy kimchi stew.
