## Ingredients
- 500 g Yukon gold potatoes
- 1 carrot, diced
- 1/2 cucumber, sliced
- 3 tbsp Japanese mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp mustard
- Salt and white pepper

## Instructions
1. Boil potatoes until tender; cube while warm.
2. Blanch carrot briefly.
3. Salt cucumber; drain excess water.
4. Mix mayo, vinegar, and mustard.
5. Fold vegetables together; season with pepper.

## Notes
Creamy Japanese-style potato salad.
