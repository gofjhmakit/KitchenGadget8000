## Ingredients
- 400 g chickpeas, drained
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp sea salt

## Instructions
1. Dry the chickpeas thoroughly.
2. Toss with oil and spices.
3. Roast at 210°C until crisp, shaking once.
4. Cool fully for maximum crunch.

## Notes
Store in a loose-lid jar.
