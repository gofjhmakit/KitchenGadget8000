## Ingredients
- 4 tbsp soy sauce
- 4 tbsp citrus juice (yuzu or lemon)
- 2 tbsp mirin
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 strip kombu
- Bonito flakes (1 tbsp)

## Instructions
1. Warm soy sauce, citrus, mirin, and vinegar.
2. Steep kombu and bonito 10 minutes.
3. Strain and cool.
4. Bottle and refrigerate.
5. Use within 1 week.

## Notes
Bright citrus soy dressing.
