## Ingredients
- 150 g cooked beetroot
- 150 g frozen berries
- 1 banana
- 250 ml kefir
- 1 tbsp oats

## Instructions
1. Blend beetroot, berries, banana, kefir, and oats until smooth.
2. Serve cold.

## Notes
Adds color and gentle sweetness.
