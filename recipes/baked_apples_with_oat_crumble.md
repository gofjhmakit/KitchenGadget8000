## Ingredients
- 4 apples
- 50 g rolled oats
- 30 g almond flour
- 20 g butter or coconut oil
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp cinnamon

## Instructions
1. Halve and core the apples.
2. Mix oats, almond flour, fat, maple syrup, and cinnamon.
3. Top apples with crumble and bake at 190°C until tender.
4. Serve warm.

## Notes
Nice with thick yogurt.
