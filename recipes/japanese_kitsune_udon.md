## Ingredients
- 400 g frozen udon
- 2 seasoned aburaage tofu pouches
- 600 ml dashi
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp mirin
- Sliced scallions
- Shichimi togarashi

## Instructions
1. Simmer aburaage in dashi, soy, and mirin 5 minutes.
2. Blanch udon in boiling water.
3. Place udon in bowls.
4. Ladle hot broth and tofu on top.
5. Garnish with scallions and shichimi.

## Notes
Sweet fox udon with fried tofu.
