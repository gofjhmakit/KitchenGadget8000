## Ingredients
- 400 g beets
- 200 g cooked green lentils
- 50 g rocket
- 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
- 40 g walnuts
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard

## Instructions
1. Roast wrapped beets at 200°C until tender, then peel and cube.
2. Whisk oil, vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper.
3. Combine beets, lentils, rocket, onion, and walnuts.
4. Dress just before serving.

## Notes
Add goat cheese for a richer version.
