## Ingredients
- 300 g turmeric rice noodles
- 200 g pork shoulder, sliced
- 1 tbsp turmeric
- 500 ml pork broth
- Peanuts
- Banana blossom
- Fresh herbs
- Rice crackers
- 2 tbsp fish sauce

## Instructions
1. Simmer pork with turmeric and fish sauce until tender.
2. Cook turmeric noodles.
3. Use minimal broth for saucy noodles.
4. Top with pork, peanuts, and banana blossom.
5. Serve with herbs and crushed rice crackers.

## Notes
Central Vietnamese turmeric noodle dish.
