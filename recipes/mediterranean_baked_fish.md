## Ingredients
- 600 g white fish fillets
- 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
- 1 onion, sliced
- 80 g olives
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Fresh basil

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion in olive oil until soft.
2. Add tomatoes, olives, and oregano; simmer 10 minutes.
3. Nestle fish into sauce.
4. Bake at 190°C for 15 minutes until fish is cooked.

## Notes
Serve with crusty bread.
