## Ingredients
- 1 aubergine, cubed
- 2 courgettes, sliced
- 2 peppers, sliced
- 400 g canned tomatoes
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 garlic cloves
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Fresh basil
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
2. Add aubergine and cook 8 minutes.
3. Add peppers, courgettes, and tomatoes.
4. Simmer 25 minutes until vegetables are tender.
5. Finish with basil.

## Notes
Provençal summer vegetable stew.
