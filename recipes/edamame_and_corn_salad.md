## Ingredients
- 300 g frozen edamame, shelled
- 200 g corn kernels
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- Sliced scallions

## Instructions
1. Blanch edamame and corn 3 minutes; cool.
2. Combine with diced bell pepper.
3. Whisk vinegar, sesame oil, and soy sauce.
4. Toss salad with dressing.
5. Chill and garnish with scallions.

## Notes
Colorful Japanese-inspired salad.
