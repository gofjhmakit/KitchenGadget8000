## Ingredients
- 400 g whole mackerel or sea bass
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- Lemongrass, minced
- Fresh mint, cilantro, and dill
- Shallots
- Bird's eye chilies
- Steamed jasmine rice

## Instructions
1. Score fish; marinate with fish sauce, lime, and lemongrass.
2. Grill or broil 8 minutes per side.
3. Mix herb salad with shallots and chilies.
4. Serve fish with herb salad and rice.
5. Drizzle extra lime.

## Notes
