## Ingredients
- 3 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
- 2 tbsp tahini
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 2 tbsp water

## Instructions
1. Grind half the sesame seeds.
2. Blend tahini, vinegar, soy, mirin, and ginger.
3. Thin with water to pourable consistency.
4. Stir in remaining sesame seeds.
5. Refrigerate up to 1 week.

## Notes
Nutty sesame salad dressing.
