## Ingredients
- 200 g rolled oats
- 500 ml milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- 200 g mixed berries

## Instructions
1. Whisk oats, milk, eggs, baking powder, cinnamon, and syrup.
2. Pour into a greased baking dish.
3. Top with berries.
4. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes until set.

## Notes
Make ahead for busy mornings.
