## Ingredients
- 400 g firm tofu, cubed
- 2 cups mixed vegetables
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 cm ginger
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Cooked brown rice

## Instructions
1. Pan-fry tofu until golden; set aside.
2. Stir-fry garlic and ginger in oil.
3. Add vegetables and cook until crisp-tender.
4. Return tofu with soy sauce and sesame oil.
5. Serve over rice.

## Notes
Quick weeknight vegan bowl.
