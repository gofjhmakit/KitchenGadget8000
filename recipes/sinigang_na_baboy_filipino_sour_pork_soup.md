## Ingredients
- 500 g pork ribs
- 1 L water
- 1 packet tamarind soup base or fresh tamarind
- 1 daikon, sliced
- 200 g string beans
- 1 tomato, quartered
- Fish sauce
- Spinach

## Instructions
1. Simmer pork ribs until tender, about 30 minutes.
2. Add tamarind, tomato, and daikon.
3. Add string beans; cook 5 minutes.
4. Season with fish sauce.
5. Finish with spinach off heat.

## Notes
Filipino sour tamarind soup.
