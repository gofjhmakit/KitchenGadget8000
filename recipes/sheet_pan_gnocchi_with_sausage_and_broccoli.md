## Ingredients
- 500 g shelf-stable gnocchi
- 300 g chicken sausage
- 1 broccoli
- 1 red onion
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp fennel seeds

## Instructions
1. Spread gnocchi, sliced sausage, broccoli, and onion on a tray.
2. Toss with oil, fennel seeds, salt, and pepper.
3. Roast at 220°C for 22-25 minutes, stirring once.
4. Finish with parmesan if desired.

## Notes
Minimal washing up.
