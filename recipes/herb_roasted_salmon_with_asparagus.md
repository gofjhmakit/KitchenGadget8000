## Ingredients
- 4 salmon fillets
- 400 g asparagus
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon
- 1 tbsp dill
- 1 garlic clove

## Instructions
1. Place salmon and asparagus on a tray.
2. Mix oil, lemon zest, dill, garlic, salt, and pepper.
3. Brush over salmon and asparagus.
4. Roast at 200°C for 14-16 minutes.

## Notes
Lovely with boiled potatoes.
