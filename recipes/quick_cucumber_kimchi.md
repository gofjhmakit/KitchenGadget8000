## Ingredients
- 4 Persian cucumbers, sliced
- 2 tbsp gochugaru
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 garlic cloves
- Scallions
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Salt cucumbers 15 minutes; drain.
2. Mix gochugaru, fish sauce, sugar, and garlic.
3. Toss cucumbers with paste.
4. Add scallions.
5. Chill 1 hour; garnish with sesame seeds.

## Notes
Fast fresh cucumber kimchi.
