## Ingredients
- 500 g chicken thigh fillets
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 180 g quinoa
- 200 g tomatoes
- 1 cucumber
- 30 g parsley
- 1 lemon

## Instructions
1. Cook quinoa and let it cool slightly.
2. Pan-sear chicken until crisp and cooked through.
3. Mix quinoa with tomato, cucumber, parsley, lemon juice, and olive oil.
4. Slice chicken and serve over the salad.

## Notes
Mint is excellent here too.
