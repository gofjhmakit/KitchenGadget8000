## Ingredients
- 250 g masoor dal
- 3 ripe tomatoes, chopped
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 8 curry leaves
- 2 green chilies, slit
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tbsp oil
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Simmer masoor dal with turmeric until soft.
2. Sauté mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chilies in oil.
3. Add tomatoes and cook until broken down.
4. Stir into dal and simmer 10 minutes.
5. Adjust salt and serve.

## Notes
Simple tomato-forward everyday dal.
