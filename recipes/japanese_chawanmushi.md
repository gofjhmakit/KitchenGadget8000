## Ingredients
- 3 eggs
- 400 ml dashi
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp mirin
- Shiitake slices
- Chicken thigh, diced
- Ginkgo nuts
- Mitsuba or scallions

## Instructions
1. Whisk eggs with dashi, soy, and mirin; strain.
2. Divide chicken, mushrooms, and ginkgo into cups.
3. Pour egg mixture through sieve.
4. Steam covered on low 15 minutes.
5. Garnish with mitsuba.

## Notes
Silky savory egg custard.
