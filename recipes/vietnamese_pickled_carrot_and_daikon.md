## Ingredients
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 200 g daikon, julienned
- 1/2 cup rice vinegar
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 tsp salt

## Instructions
1. Massage carrot and daikon with salt 15 minutes.
2. Rinse and squeeze dry.
3. Dissolve sugar in vinegar.
4. Cover vegetables with pickling liquid.
5. Refrigerate 2 hours before use.

## Notes
Do chua banh mi pickles.
