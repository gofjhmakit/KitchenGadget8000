## Ingredients
- 150 g mung bean vermicelli
- 200 g ground pork
- 2 tbsp doubanjiang
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- 2 garlic cloves
- 500 ml chicken broth
- Sliced scallions
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Soak vermicelli 10 minutes.
2. Brown pork with garlic and doubanjiang.
3. Add broth, soy sauce, and wine; bring to simmer.
4. Add vermicelli; cook until broth is absorbed.
5. Garnish with scallions.

## Notes
Sichuan minced pork over glass noodles.
