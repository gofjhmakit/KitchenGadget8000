## Ingredients
- to taste Salt and pepper
- 1 lb, trimmed Green beans
- 1/4 cup Ketchup
- >
- 3 lbs russet potatoes
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Remove the meatloaf from packaging and place on a foil-lined sheet pan or in the provided tray. If you want extra glaze, brush the top with ketchup.
3. Bake for 55-65 minutes until internal temperature reaches 160°F.
4. While meatloaf bakes, peel and cube the potatoes. Boil in salted water for 15-18 minutes until fork-tender.
5. Drain potatoes. Add butter and heavy cream. Mash to your preferred texture. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Let the meatloaf rest 10 minutes before slicing. Serve with mashed potatoes.
