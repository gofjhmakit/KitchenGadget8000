## Ingredients
- 400 ml coconut milk
- 500 ml vegetable broth
- 300 g mixed mushrooms
- 2 stalks lemongrass
- 4 kaffir lime leaves
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- Thai basil

## Instructions
1. Simmer broth with lemongrass and lime leaves.
2. Add mushrooms; cook 5 minutes.
3. Stir in coconut milk gently.
4. Season with soy and lime.
5. Finish with Thai basil.

## Notes
Plant-based tom kha style soup.
