## Ingredients
- 1 cup fresh mint leaves
- 1/2 cup cilantro
- 1 green chili
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- Salt
- 2 tbsp water

## Instructions
1. Blend mint, cilantro, chili, lime, and water.
2. Toast and grind cumin; stir in.
3. Season with salt.
4. Thin to dressing consistency.
5. Use on salads and grilled proteins.

## Notes
Fresh herb Indian dressing.
