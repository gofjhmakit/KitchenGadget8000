## Ingredients
- 2 bananas
- 1 cup milk
- Ice cubes
- 3 cups frozen three berry blend
- 1 cup greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp honey

## Instructions
1. Add frozen berries, Greek yogurt, bananas, honey, and milk to a blender.
2. Blend on high for 60 seconds until smooth and creamy.
3. If it's too thick, add a splash more milk. Too thin, add a handful of ice or more frozen berries.
4. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
5. Feel virtuous about your life choices for at least 20 minutes.
