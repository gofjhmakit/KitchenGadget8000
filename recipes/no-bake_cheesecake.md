## Ingredients
- 6 tbsp Unsalted butter, melted
- 1 cup Granulated sugar
- 2 tsp Pure vanilla extract
- 1 tbsp Fresh lemon juice
- Pinch of salt
- 3 blocks cream cheese 4-pack
- 1.5 cups heavy whipping cream
- 1 sleeve + 2 sheets honey maid graham crackers

## Instructions
1. Crush the graham crackers into fine crumbs — you need about 2 cups. Mix with the melted butter and 2 tablespoons of sugar until it feels like wet sand.
2. Press the crust firmly into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan, pushing it slightly up the sides. Refrigerate while you make the filling.
3. Beat the cream cheese with a hand mixer or stand mixer until completely smooth, about 3 minutes. Scrape down the bowl often — lumps are the enemy here.
4. Add the remaining sugar, vanilla, lemon juice, and salt. Beat until combined, another 2 minutes.
5. In a separate cold bowl, whip the heavy cream to stiff peaks. This takes about 3-4 minutes on high speed.
6. Fold one-third of the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture to lighten it. Then gently fold in the remaining whipped cream, being careful not to deflate it.
7. Pour the filling over the crust and smooth the top with an offset spatula or the back of a spoon.
8. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 6 hours, or overnight for best results.
9. Release the springform ring, slice with a knife dipped in hot water, and serve.
