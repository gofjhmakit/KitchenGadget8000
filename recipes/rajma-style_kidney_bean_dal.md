## Ingredients
- 250 g red kidney beans
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 tomatoes, pureed
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp cumin powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 2 tbsp oil
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Soak kidney beans overnight; simmer until tender.
2. Sauté onion in oil until golden.
3. Add ginger-garlic and spice powders; cook 2 minutes.
4. Add tomato puree and simmer 10 minutes.
5. Stir in beans and cook 15 minutes; garnish with cilantro.

## Notes
North Indian kidney bean curry.
