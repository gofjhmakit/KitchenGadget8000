## Ingredients
- 250 g pearl barley
- 300 g mushrooms
- 1 onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 900 ml vegetable stock
- 30 g parmesan
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion, garlic, and mushrooms.
2. Add barley and toast briefly.
3. Add stock in batches, stirring occasionally, until the barley is tender.
4. Finish with parmesan and black pepper.

## Notes
Earthy and satisfying.
