## Ingredients
- 400 g spinach
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 garlic clove
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Blanch spinach 30 seconds; squeeze dry.
2. Toss with soy, sesame oil, and garlic.
3. Season lightly with salt.
4. Form into small mounds.
5. Sprinkle sesame seeds.

## Notes
Essential Korean banchan.
