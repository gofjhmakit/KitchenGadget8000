## Ingredients
- 30 g dried hijiki
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 100 g aburaage, sliced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Rehydrate hijiki; drain.
2. Simmer hijiki, carrot, and aburaage with soy and mirin 10 minutes.
3. Cool to room temperature.
4. Toss with sesame oil.
5. Sprinkle sesame seeds.

## Notes
Mineral-rich seaweed side.
