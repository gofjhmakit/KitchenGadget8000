## Ingredients
- 300 g alkaline wheat noodles
- 2 tbsp sesame paste
- 1 tbsp chili oil
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp black vinegar
- Pickled mustard stems, chopped
- Sliced scallions

## Instructions
1. Cook alkaline noodles; drain well.
2. Whisk sesame paste with soy, vinegar, and chili oil.
3. Toss hot noodles vigorously with sauce.
4. Top with pickled mustard stems.
5. Garnish with scallions.

## Notes
Wuhan sesame chili dry noodles.
