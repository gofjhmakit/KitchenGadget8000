## Ingredients
- 180 g quinoa
- 1 fennel bulb, thinly sliced
- 2 oranges, segmented
- 60 g baby spinach
- 40 g toasted almonds
- 30 g feta
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Cook quinoa until tender and cool slightly.
2. Whisk olive oil and lemon juice with a pinch of salt.
3. Toss quinoa, fennel, orange, spinach, and almonds with dressing.
4. Finish with feta and extra black pepper.

## Notes
Excellent with grilled salmon.
