## Ingredients
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 3 tbsp neutral oil
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp grated onion
- 1 tsp sugar
- White pepper

## Instructions
1. Grate onion and squeeze out excess liquid.
2. Whisk soy, oil, vinegar, sugar, and onion.
3. Season with white pepper.
4. Rest 15 minutes.
5. Classic Japanese Western-style dressing.

## Notes
All-purpose wafu salad dressing.
