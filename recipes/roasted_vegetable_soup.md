## Ingredients
- 2 carrots, chopped
- 2 parsnips, chopped
- 1 sweet potato, cubed
- 1 onion, quartered
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 800 ml vegetable broth
- 1 tsp thyme
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Toss vegetables with oil, thyme, salt, and pepper.
2. Roast at 200°C for 25 minutes.
3. Simmer roasted vegetables in broth 10 minutes.
4. Blend until smooth and adjust seasoning.

## Notes
Naturally sweet and filling.
