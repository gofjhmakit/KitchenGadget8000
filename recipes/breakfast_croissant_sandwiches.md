## Ingredients
- to taste Salt and pepper
- 1 sliced Avocado
- to taste Hot sauce
- 6 croissants butter croissants
- 12 slices thick-sliced bacon
- 6 eggs large eggs
- >

## Instructions
1. Cook bacon in a skillet until crispy. Set aside on paper towels.
2. In the same pan (drain most of the grease), cook eggs to your preference — fried, scrambled, or as thin omelets that fit the croissant.
3. Slice croissants in half horizontally.
4. Layer: bottom croissant half, cheese, egg, 2 slices bacon, avocado if using, top half.
5. Eat immediately, or wrap in foil and bake at 350°F for 5 minutes to melt the cheese and warm through.
