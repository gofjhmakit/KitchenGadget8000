time: 30 min
servings: 4
tags: [soup, vegetarian]
image: 🍲
---

## Ingredients
- 500 g broccoli florets
- 1 leek, sliced
- 1 potato, diced
- 750 ml vegetable stock
- 40 g parmesan
- 100 ml milk
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Cook leek in olive oil until soft.
2. Add potato, broccoli, and stock
3.  simmer until tender.
4. Blend smooth, then stir in milk and parmesan.
5. Season generously with pepper.

## Notes
Finish with lemon for balance.
