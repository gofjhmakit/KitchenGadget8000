time: 30 min
servings: 4
tags: [soup, vegetarian, vegan]

---

## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves
- 800 g chopped tomatoes
- 400 g white beans
- 500 ml vegetable stock
- 1 tsp smoked paprika

## Instructions
1. Cook onion and garlic in oil until soft.
2. Add tomatoes, beans, stock, and paprika.
3. Simmer 15 minutes.
4. Blend half the soup for body, then return to the pot.

## Notes
Serve with basil and olive oil.
