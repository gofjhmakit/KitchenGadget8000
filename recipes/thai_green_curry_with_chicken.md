## Ingredients
- 400 g chicken thigh, cubed
- 3 tbsp green curry paste
- 400 ml coconut milk
- Thai eggplant
- Bamboo shoots
- Thai basil
- Fish sauce
- Palm sugar
- Jasmine rice

## Instructions
1. Fry curry paste in thick coconut cream 2 minutes.
2. Add chicken; cook 5 minutes.
3. Add remaining coconut milk and vegetables.
4. Simmer 12 minutes.
5. Season with fish sauce and palm sugar; finish with basil.

## Notes
Fragrant coconut green curry.
