## Ingredients
- 1 mango, cubed
- 1 pineapple, cubed
- 1 jicama, cubed
- 2 tbsp tamarind paste
- 1 tbsp palm sugar
- 1 tbsp rojak sauce
- Roasted peanuts
- Torch ginger bud, sliced

## Instructions
1. Cut all fruit into bite cubes.
2. Whisk tamarind, palm sugar, and rojak sauce.
3. Toss fruit gently with dressing.
4. Plate and scatter peanuts.
5. Top with torch ginger if available.

## Notes
Tropical Malaysian fruit salad.
