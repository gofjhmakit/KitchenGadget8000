## Ingredients
- 2 Japanese eggplants, halved
- 2 tbsp white miso
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Scallions

## Instructions
1. Score eggplant halves and pan-sear until tender.
2. Whisk miso, mirin, and sugar.
3. Broil eggplant with miso glaze 3 minutes.
4. Drizzle sesame oil.
5. Garnish with sesame seeds and scallions.

## Notes
Sweet miso glazed eggplant.
