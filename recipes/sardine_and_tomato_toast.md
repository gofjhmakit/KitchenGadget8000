## Ingredients
- 2 cans sardines in olive oil
- 2 slices sourdough
- 1 tomato, sliced
- 1 lemon
- Fresh parsley
- Black pepper

## Instructions
1. Toast bread.
2. Top with tomato slices.
3. Lay sardines over tomatoes.
4. Finish with lemon, parsley, and pepper.

## Notes
Quick omega-3 rich lunch.
