## Ingredients
- 200 g lotus root, thinly sliced
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- Fresh Vietnamese coriander
- Crushed peanuts

## Instructions
1. Blanch lotus root slices 2 minutes; cool in vinegar water.
2. Combine with carrot.
3. Whisk vinegar, sugar, and soy sauce.
4. Toss with herbs.
5. Top with peanuts.

## Notes
Crunchy lotus root salad.
