## Ingredients
- 500 g chicken thighs
- 200 g long-grain rice
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 1.8 L chicken broth
- 1 bay leaf
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Simmer chicken in broth with bay leaf until cooked; shred meat.
2. Sauté onion, carrot, and celery; return to pot.
3. Add rice and simmer 15 minutes until tender.
4. Return shredded chicken, season, and serve.

## Notes
Simple restorative soup.
