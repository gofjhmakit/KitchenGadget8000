## Ingredients
- 600 g pork belly, cubed
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 3 garlic cloves
- Coconut water (200 ml)
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Jasmine rice

## Instructions
1. Caramelize sugar until amber.
2. Add pork and garlic; brown.
3. Add fish sauce and coconut water.
4. Simmer covered 45 minutes.
5. Add eggs last 10 minutes; serve with rice.

## Notes
Slow-braised Vietnamese pork.
