## Ingredients
- 2 kiwis
- 1 handful spinach
- 1 banana
- 250 ml cold water
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- juice of 1/2 lime

## Instructions
1. Peel the kiwis.
2. Blend all ingredients until silky smooth.
3. Serve immediately.

## Notes
Use frozen banana for extra chill.
