## Ingredients
- 400 g soybean sprouts
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp gochugaru
- Scallions
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Blanch sprouts 3 minutes; drain well.
2. Mix garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, and gochugaru.
3. Toss warm sprouts with seasoning.
4. Garnish with scallions and sesame seeds.
5. Serve warm or chilled.

## Notes
Everyday Korean bean sprout banchan.
