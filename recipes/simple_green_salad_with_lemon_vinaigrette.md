## Ingredients
- 200 g mixed salad leaves
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 200 g cherry tomatoes
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Wash and dry salad leaves.
2. Whisk olive oil, lemon, mustard, salt, and pepper.
3. Toss vegetables with dressing just before serving.

## Notes
Everyday side salad.
