## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp tamarind paste
- 2 tbsp palm sugar
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- Bird's eye chili, minced

## Instructions
1. Dissolve tamarind and palm sugar in 3 tbsp warm water.
2. Add fish sauce, lime, garlic, and chili.
3. Simmer 3 minutes until glossy.
4. Cool before use.
5. Perfect for papaya salad.

## Notes
Sweet-sour tamarind dressing.
