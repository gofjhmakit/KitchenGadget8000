## Ingredients
- 500 g small potatoes, halved
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 garlic cloves
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Scallions

## Instructions
1. Par-boil potatoes 5 minutes.
2. Simmer with soy sauce, maple syrup, garlic, and splash of water.
3. Reduce until glaze coats potatoes.
4. Drizzle sesame oil.
5. Garnish with scallions and sesame seeds.

## Notes
Sweet soy braised Korean potatoes.
