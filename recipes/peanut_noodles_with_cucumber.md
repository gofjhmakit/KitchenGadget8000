## Ingredients
- 300 g whole wheat noodles
- 3 tbsp natural peanut butter
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 1 cucumber, julienned
- Sliced scallions
- Crushed peanuts

## Instructions
1. Cook noodles; rinse cold.
2. Whisk peanut butter with soy sauce, vinegar, ginger, and 3 tbsp warm water.
3. Toss noodles with peanut sauce.
4. Top with cucumber and scallions.
5. Sprinkle crushed peanuts.

## Notes
Protein-rich cold peanut noodles.
