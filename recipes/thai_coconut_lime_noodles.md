## Ingredients
- 250 g rice noodles
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- Snow peas
- Fresh basil
- Red chili slices

## Instructions
1. Cook rice noodles; drain.
2. Warm coconut milk with ginger.
3. Add soy sauce and lime juice.
4. Toss noodles with coconut lime sauce.
5. Top with snow peas, basil, and chili.

## Notes
Creamy tangy Thai noodle bowl.
