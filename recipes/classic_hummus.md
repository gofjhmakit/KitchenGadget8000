## Ingredients
- 400 g canned chickpeas, drained
- 3 tbsp tahini
- 2 garlic cloves
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- Salt
- 2-4 tbsp cold water

## Instructions
1. Blend chickpeas, tahini, garlic, lemon juice, cumin, and salt until smooth.
2. Stream in olive oil and water to reach a creamy texture.
3. Adjust seasoning and serve with vegetables or pita.

## Notes
Middle Eastern staple dip.
