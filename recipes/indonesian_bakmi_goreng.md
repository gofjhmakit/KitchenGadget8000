## Ingredients
- 300 g fresh egg bakmi noodles
- 200 g chicken, diced
- 2 tbsp kecap manis
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- Pak choi
- Fried shallots
- Pickled cucumber
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Boil bakmi briefly; drain.
2. Stir-fry chicken until cooked.
3. Add noodles, kecap manis, and oyster sauce.
4. Wok-fry over high heat 3 minutes.
5. Serve with pak choi, shallots, and pickles.

## Notes
Indonesian sweet soy fried noodles.
