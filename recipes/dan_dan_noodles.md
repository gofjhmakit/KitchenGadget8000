## Ingredients
- 250 g fresh wheat noodles
- 150 g ground pork
- 2 tbsp Sichuan chili oil
- 1 tbsp Chinese sesame paste
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp black vinegar
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorns, ground
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Sliced scallions

## Instructions
1. Cook noodles until al dente; drain and divide into bowls.
2. Brown pork with garlic in oil until crisp.
3. Whisk sesame paste, soy sauce, vinegar, and chili oil with 3 tbsp noodle water.
4. Spoon sauce over noodles; top with pork and scallions.
5. Finish with ground Sichuan pepper.

## Notes
Spicy Sichuan street noodle bowl.
