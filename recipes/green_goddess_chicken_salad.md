## Ingredients
- 300 g cooked chicken breast, sliced
- 1 romaine lettuce
- 100 g cucumber
- 120 g avocado
- 30 g chives
- 20 g parsley
- 120 g Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Blend yogurt, avocado, herbs, lemon juice, salt, and a splash of water into a creamy dressing.
2. Arrange lettuce, chicken, and cucumber on a platter.
3. Spoon over dressing and serve chilled.

## Notes
Great in wraps the next day.
