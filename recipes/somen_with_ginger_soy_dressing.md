## Ingredients
- 200 g somen noodles
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- Shredded nori
- Sliced scallions
- Ice water bath

## Instructions
1. Cook somen 2 minutes; plunge into ice water.
2. Mix soy sauce, mirin, and ginger.
3. Drain somen well.
4. Drizzle ginger soy dressing.
5. Top with nori and scallions.

## Notes
Chilled fine Japanese wheat noodles.
