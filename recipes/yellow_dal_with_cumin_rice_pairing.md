## Ingredients
- 250 g yellow moong dal
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 green chili, minced
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tbsp ghee
- Fresh lemon juice
- Salt
- Fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Simmer dal with turmeric and salt until smooth.
2. Heat ghee; toast cumin and green chili.
3. Pour tempering over dal.
4. Add lemon juice.
5. Garnish with cilantro and serve with jeera rice.

## Notes
Minimal weeknight yellow dal.
