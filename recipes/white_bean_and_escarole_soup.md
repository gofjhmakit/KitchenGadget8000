## Ingredients
- 400 g cannellini beans
- 1 head escarole, chopped
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves
- 800 ml vegetable broth
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp rosemary
- Parmesan rind (optional)

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
2. Add broth, beans, and rosemary; simmer 10 minutes.
3. Stir in escarole until wilted.
4. Simmer 8 minutes, season, and serve.

## Notes
Rustic Italian soup.
