## Ingredients
- 3 tbsp calamansi or lime juice
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp coconut vinegar
- 1 tsp honey
- Salt and pepper
- Minced shallot

## Instructions
1. Whisk calamansi juice with vinegar and honey.
2. Stream in olive oil while whisking.
3. Add shallot, salt, and pepper.
4. Rest 10 minutes.
5. Bright dressing for tropical salads.

## Notes
Filipino citrus salad dressing.
