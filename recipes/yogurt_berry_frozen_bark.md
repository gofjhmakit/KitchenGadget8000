time: 15 min

---

## Ingredients
- 300 g Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp honey
- 120 g mixed berries
- 20 g pistachios

## Instructions
1. Mix yogurt with honey.
2. Spread on a lined tray and scatter berries and pistachios.
3. Freeze until solid, then break into shards.

## Notes
Keep frozen until serving.
