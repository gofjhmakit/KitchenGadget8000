## Ingredients
- 300 g fresh wheat noodles
- 4 scallions, finely sliced
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp neutral oil
- 1 tsp sugar
- White pepper

## Instructions
1. Steam noodles until just cooked.
2. Heat oil until smoking; pour over scallions.
3. Add soy sauce and sugar to scallion oil.
4. Toss steamed noodles with sauce.
5. Season with white pepper.

## Notes
Minimal Cantonese steamed noodles.
