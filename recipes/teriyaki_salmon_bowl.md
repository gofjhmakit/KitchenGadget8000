## Ingredients
- 2 salmon fillets (150 g each)
- 3 tbsp teriyaki sauce
- 300 g cooked short-grain rice
- Steamed broccoli
- Pickled ginger
- Sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Pan-sear salmon skin-side down 4 minutes.
2. Flip; glaze with teriyaki and cook 3 minutes.
3. Steam broccoli.
4. Serve salmon over rice with broccoli.
5. Garnish with ginger and sesame seeds.

## Notes
Balanced Japanese salmon rice bowl.
