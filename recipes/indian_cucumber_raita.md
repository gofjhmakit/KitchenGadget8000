## Ingredients
- 400 g cucumber, grated
- 250 g plain yogurt
- 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
- 2 tbsp chopped mint
- 1/2 tsp chaat masala
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Grate cucumber and squeeze out excess water.
2. Whisk yogurt until smooth.
3. Fold in cucumber, cumin, mint, and chaat masala.
4. Season with salt.
5. Chill 15 minutes before serving with dal or curry.

## Notes
Cooling yogurt side for spicy meals.
