## Ingredients
- 2 cans tuna in water, drained
- 400 g cannellini beans, rinsed
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Black pepper

## Instructions
1. Combine tuna and beans in a bowl.
2. Add onion, olive oil, vinegar, and oregano.
3. Toss gently and season with pepper.
4. Serve on greens or with bread.

## Notes
Italian pantry lunch.
