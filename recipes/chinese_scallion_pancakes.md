## Ingredients
- 300 g all-purpose flour
- 180 ml hot water
- 4 scallions, sliced
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- Salt
- Oil for frying

## Instructions
1. Knead flour and water into soft dough; rest 20 minutes.
2. Roll dough thin; brush sesame oil and scatter scallions.
3. Roll up and coil; flatten again.
4. Pan-fry in oil until golden and flaky.
5. Cut into wedges.

## Notes
Crispy layered scallion flatbread.
