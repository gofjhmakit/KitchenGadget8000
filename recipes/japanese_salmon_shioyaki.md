## Ingredients
- 2 salmon fillets
- 1 tsp salt
- Lemon wedges
- Grated daikon
- Steamed rice
- Miso soup (optional side)

## Instructions
1. Salt salmon generously; rest 20 minutes.
2. Pat dry and grill or pan-sear skin-side down.
3. Cook until medium inside.
4. Serve with grated daikon and lemon.
5. Pair with rice.

## Notes
Simple salt-grilled salmon.
