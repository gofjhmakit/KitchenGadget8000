## Ingredients
- 800 g baby potatoes
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 120 g Greek yogurt
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tbsp dill

## Instructions
1. Boil potatoes until tender.
2. Smash on a tray, drizzle with oil, and roast at 220°C until crisp.
3. Mix yogurt with garlic and dill.
4. Serve potatoes with the yogurt spooned underneath.

## Notes
Finish with flaky salt.
