## Ingredients
- 4 bell peppers
- 250 g ricotta
- 120 g spinach
- 80 g cooked rice
- 40 g parmesan
- 1 garlic clove

## Instructions
1. Halve and seed the peppers.
2. Mix ricotta, spinach, rice, parmesan, and garlic.
3. Fill peppers and bake at 200°C for 30 minutes.
4. Broil briefly for extra color.

## Notes
Works with cooked quinoa too.
