## Ingredients
- 4 tbsp peanut butter
- 2 tbsp kecap manis
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tsp sambal oelek
- 100 ml warm water
- 1 garlic clove, minced

## Instructions
1. Whisk peanut butter with warm water until smooth.
2. Add kecap manis, lime, sambal, and garlic.
3. Simmer gently 5 minutes.
4. Cool to room temperature.
5. Thin with water if needed.

## Notes
Rich peanut dressing for gado gado.
