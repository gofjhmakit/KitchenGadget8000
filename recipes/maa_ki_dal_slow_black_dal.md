## Ingredients
- 250 g whole black urad dal
- 1 onion, sliced
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 200 g crushed tomatoes
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 2 tbsp butter
- Fresh cream (2 tbsp, optional)
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Simmer urad dal with salt until very soft, about 45 minutes.
2. Sauté onion in butter until caramelized.
3. Add ginger-garlic and tomatoes; cook 10 minutes.
4. Combine with dal and simmer 20 minutes.
5. Finish with garam masala and a swirl of cream.

## Notes
Creamy black dal without heavy cream.
