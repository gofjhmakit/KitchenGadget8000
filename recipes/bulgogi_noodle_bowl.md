## Ingredients
- 300 g sweet potato glass noodles
- 250 g bulgogi beef, sliced
- 100 g spinach
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Marinate beef with soy sauce, sugar, and sesame oil 15 minutes.
2. Stir-fry beef until caramelized.
3. Boil glass noodles; drain.
4. Blanch spinach and carrot.
5. Toss noodles with vegetables and beef; garnish with sesame seeds.

## Notes
Korean BBQ beef over glass noodles.
