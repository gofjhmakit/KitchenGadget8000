## Ingredients
- 300 g fresh wheat noodles
- 3 tbsp chili oil
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp black vinegar
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorn powder
- Sliced cucumber
- Crushed roasted peanuts

## Instructions
1. Cook noodles; drain and cool slightly.
2. Whisk chili oil, soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.
3. Toss noodles with sauce.
4. Top with cucumber and peanuts.
5. Dust with Sichuan pepper powder.

## Notes
Fiery numbing cold noodle bowl.
