## Ingredients
- 6 eggs
- 60 ml milk
- 80 g spinach, chopped
- 50 g feta
- 1 spring onion
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
2. Fold in spinach, feta, and onion.
3. Divide into an oiled muffin tray.
4. Bake at 190°C for 15-18 minutes.

## Notes
Great warm or cold.
