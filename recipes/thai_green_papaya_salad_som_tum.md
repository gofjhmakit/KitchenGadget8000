## Ingredients
- 1 green papaya, shredded
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp palm sugar
- 2 bird's eye chilies
- 100 g green beans, halved
- Cherry tomatoes
- Roasted peanuts

## Instructions
1. Pound chilies and palm sugar in mortar.
2. Add green beans and tomatoes; bruise lightly.
3. Toss shredded papaya with fish sauce and lime.
4. Pound briefly to combine flavors.
5. Serve topped with peanuts.

## Notes
Spicy shredded papaya salad.
