## Ingredients
- 4 Persian cucumbers
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp black vinegar
- 2 tbsp chili oil
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- Sliced scallions
- Cilantro

## Instructions
1. Smash cucumbers with rolling pin; cut into chunks.
2. Salt lightly; rest 10 minutes; drain.
3. Whisk soy sauce, black vinegar, and chili oil.
4. Toss cucumbers with garlic and dressing.
5. Garnish with scallions and cilantro.

## Notes
Sichuan-style smashed cucumber.
