## Ingredients
- 150 g glass noodles
- 200 g shrimp, poached
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp palm sugar
- Cherry tomatoes, halved
- Fresh mint
- Roasted peanuts
- Sliced shallots

## Instructions
1. Soak and blanch glass noodles; cool.
2. Whisk fish sauce, lime, and palm sugar.
3. Toss noodles with shrimp, tomatoes, and shallots.
4. Dress and fold in mint.
5. Top with peanuts.

## Notes
Bright Thai warm noodle salad.
