## Ingredients
- 500 g potatoes, thinly sliced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 6 eggs
- 100 ml olive oil
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Gently fry potatoes and onion in olive oil until tender.
2. Drain excess oil and cool slightly.
3. Beat eggs with salt; mix with potatoes.
4. Cook in a nonstick pan 8 minutes per side until set.

## Notes
Classic potato omelette.
