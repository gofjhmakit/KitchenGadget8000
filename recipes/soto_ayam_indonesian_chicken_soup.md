## Ingredients
- 1 whole chicken
- 1 L water
- 2 lemongrass stalks
- Turmeric
- Galangal
- Rice or glass noodles
- Bean sprouts
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Fried shallots
- Lime

## Instructions
1. Simmer chicken with lemongrass, turmeric, and galangal until cooked.
2. Shred chicken; strain golden broth.
3. Blanch noodles.
4. Assemble bowls with chicken, noodles, and sprouts.
5. Top with egg, shallots, and lime.

## Notes
Bright Indonesian chicken soup.
