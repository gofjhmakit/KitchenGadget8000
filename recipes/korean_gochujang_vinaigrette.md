## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp gochujang
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 garlic clove, minced

## Instructions
1. Whisk gochujang with vinegar until smooth.
2. Add sesame oil, soy sauce, sugar, and garlic.
3. Emulsify with fork or blender.
4. Rest 10 minutes.
5. Store refrigerated 1 week.

## Notes
Spicy Korean salad dressing.
