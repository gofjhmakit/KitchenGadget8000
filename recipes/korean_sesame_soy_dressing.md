## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Whisk soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, and sugar.
2. Add minced garlic.
3. Sprinkle sesame seeds before serving.
4. Use on blanched vegetables.
5. Store refrigerated 5 days.

## Notes
Simple Korean banchan dressing.
