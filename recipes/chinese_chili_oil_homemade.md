## Ingredients
- 100 ml neutral oil
- 3 tbsp chili flakes
- 1 tbsp Sichuan peppercorns
- 4 star anise
- 4 garlic cloves, sliced
- 1 inch ginger, sliced
- 1 tsp salt

## Instructions
1. Heat aromatics in oil until garlic is golden; remove solids.
2. Pour hot oil over chili flakes and peppercorns.
3. Stir in salt.
4. Cool completely.
5. Store in jar at room temperature 1 month.

## Notes
Fragrant Sichuan chili oil.
