## Ingredients
- 250 g moong dal
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 8 curry leaves
- 1 dried red chili
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Simmer moong dal with turmeric and water until soft.
2. Stir in coconut milk and simmer 8 minutes.
3. Temper mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chili in coconut oil.
4. Pour over dal.
5. Serve with appam or rice.

## Notes
Kerala-style coconut dal.
