## Ingredients
- 300 g green papaya, julienned
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- Ginger slices
- Garlic cloves

## Instructions
1. Salt papaya and carrot 30 minutes; rinse.
2. Simmer vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved.
3. Pack vegetables with ginger and garlic in jar.
4. Pour hot brine over.
5. Refrigerate 24 hours before serving.

## Notes
Sweet Filipino pickle relish.
