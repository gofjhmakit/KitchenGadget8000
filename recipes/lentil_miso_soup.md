## Ingredients
- 150 g red lentils
- 800 ml vegetable broth
- 2 tbsp white miso
- 1 carrot, diced
- 2 cm ginger, grated
- Scallions
- Nori strips

## Instructions
1. Simmer lentils with carrot and ginger in broth until soft.
2. Partially blend for creamy texture.
3. Whisk miso in ladle of broth; stir in.
4. Heat gently without boiling.
5. Top with scallions and nori.

## Notes
Fusion protein-rich miso lentil soup.
