## Ingredients
- 3 Persian cucumbers, sliced
- 2 tbsp gochugaru
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp sugar
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Salt cucumbers lightly; rest 10 minutes; drain.
2. Mix gochugaru, vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, and garlic.
3. Toss cucumbers with spicy dressing.
4. Chill 15 minutes.
5. Sprinkle sesame seeds before serving.

## Notes
Spicy Korean cucumber banchan.
