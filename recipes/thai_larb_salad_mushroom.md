## Ingredients
- 400 g oyster mushrooms, minced
- 3 tbsp lime juice
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp toasted rice powder
- Shallots, sliced
- Fresh mint and cilantro
- Lettuce cups
- Bird's eye chilies

## Instructions
1. Dry-toast rice until golden; grind to powder.
2. Sauté mushrooms until lightly browned; cool.
3. Toss mushrooms with lime, soy, shallots, and chilies.
4. Add herbs and rice powder.
5. Serve in lettuce cups.

## Notes
Vegan Isaan-style larb.
