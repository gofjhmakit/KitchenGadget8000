## Ingredients
- 250 g split yellow lentils
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 2 cm ginger
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tbsp oil
- 800 ml water
- Fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Rinse lentils and simmer with turmeric and water until soft, about 25 minutes.
2. Sauté onion, ginger, and garlic in oil until golden.
3. Add tomatoes and cook until broken down.
4. Stir tempered aromatics into lentils and simmer 5 minutes.
5. Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice.

## Notes
Everyday Indian lentil staple.
