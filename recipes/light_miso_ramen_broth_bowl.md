## Ingredients
- 800 ml dashi
- 2 tbsp red miso
- 200 g fresh ramen noodles
- Soft-boiled egg
- Corn kernels
- Nori
- Scallions
- Bamboo shoots

## Instructions
1. Simmer dashi; whisk in miso off boil.
2. Cook ramen noodles separately.
3. Place noodles in bowls.
4. Ladle miso broth over.
5. Top with egg, corn, nori, scallions, and bamboo.

## Notes
Quick home miso ramen.
