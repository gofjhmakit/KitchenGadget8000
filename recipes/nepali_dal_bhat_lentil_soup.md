## Ingredients
- 250 g yellow split peas
- 1 tomato, diced
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 2 dried chilies
- 2 tbsp ghee
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Simmer split peas with turmeric and salt until smooth.
2. Heat ghee; toast cumin and dried chilies.
3. Add tomato to tempering; cook 3 minutes.
4. Stir into dal and simmer 8 minutes.
5. Serve with rice and cilantro.

## Notes
Everyday Nepali lentil component of dal bhat.
