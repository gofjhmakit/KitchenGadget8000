## Ingredients
- 3 celery stalks, thinly sliced
- 200 g firm tofu, julienned and pan-fried
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp black vinegar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- Sliced scallions
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Pan-fry tofu strips until golden; cool.
2. Blanch celery 30 seconds; cool.
3. Whisk soy, vinegar, and sesame oil.
4. Toss celery and tofu with dressing.
5. Garnish with scallions and sesame seeds.

## Notes
Crunchy tofu celery salad.
