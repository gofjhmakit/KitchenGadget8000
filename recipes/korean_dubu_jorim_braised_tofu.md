## Ingredients
- 400 g firm tofu, sliced
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp gochugaru
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 2 garlic cloves
- Scallions
- Sesame seeds
- Sliced onion

## Instructions
1. Pan-fry tofu until golden.
2. Simmer with soy, gochugaru, maple syrup, garlic, and onion.
3. Reduce until sauce glazes tofu.
4. Garnish with scallions and sesame.
5. Serve with rice.

## Notes
Spicy-sweet braised tofu.
