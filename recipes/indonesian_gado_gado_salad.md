## Ingredients
- 200 g green beans, blanched
- 200 g cabbage, shredded
- 2 boiled eggs, halved
- 200 g firm tofu, fried
- 150 g bean sprouts
- Peanut sauce (4 tbsp peanut butter, 2 tbsp kecap manis, lime)
- Krupuk crackers

## Instructions
1. Blanch all vegetables separately.
2. Pan-fry tofu until golden.
3. Whisk peanut butter, kecap manis, and lime into sauce.
4. Arrange vegetables and tofu on platter.
5. Drizzle peanut sauce; serve with krupuk.

## Notes
Indonesian vegetable salad with peanut dressing.
