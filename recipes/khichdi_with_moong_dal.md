## Ingredients
- 150 g moong dal
- 150 g basmati rice
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 cm ginger, grated
- 1 tbsp ghee
- 900 ml water
- Salt
- Fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Rinse rice and dal together.
2. Heat ghee; add cumin and ginger.
3. Add rice, dal, turmeric, water, and salt.
4. Simmer covered on low 25 minutes until soft and porridge-like.
5. Rest 5 minutes, fluff, and garnish with cilantro.

## Notes
Comfort one-pot rice and lentil dish.
