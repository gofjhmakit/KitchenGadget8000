## Ingredients
- 200 g dried soba noodles
- 400 ml mentsuyu (noodle soup base)
- 1 sheet nori, shredded
- Wasabi
- Sliced scallions
- Grated daikon

## Instructions
1. Cook soba according to package; rinse cold.
2. Warm mentsuyu with 200 ml water.
3. Divide soba into bowls.
4. Pour hot tsuyu over noodles.
5. Top with nori, scallions, wasabi, and daikon.

## Notes
Simple hot soba in broth.
