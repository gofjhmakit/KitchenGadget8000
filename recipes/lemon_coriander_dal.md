## Ingredients
- 250 g masoor dal
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Large bunch fresh coriander, chopped
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 2 dried red chilies
- 2 tbsp oil
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Simmer masoor dal with turmeric and salt until creamy.
2. Heat oil; toast cumin and dried chilies.
3. Pour tempering over dal.
4. Stir in lemon juice and most of the coriander.
5. Serve garnished with remaining coriander.

## Notes
Bright tangy weeknight dal.
