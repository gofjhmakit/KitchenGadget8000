## Ingredients
- 200 g wide rice noodles
- 200 g chicken thigh, sliced
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- Thai basil leaves
- 2 bird's eye chilies
- 3 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Soak wide rice noodles until pliable.
2. Stir-fry garlic and chilies in oil.
3. Add chicken; cook until done.
4. Toss noodles with oyster sauce, fish sauce, and soy sauce.
5. Finish with Thai basil off heat.

## Notes
Spicy basil rice noodle stir fry.
