## Ingredients
- 6 eggs
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 star anise
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 2 tbsp black tea leaves
- 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorns

## Instructions
1. Hard-boil eggs; crack shells gently all over.
2. Simmer soy, spices, tea, and water.
3. Add eggs; simmer 30 minutes.
4. Steep off heat 1 hour.
5. Peel to reveal marble pattern.

## Notes
Aromatic Chinese marbled eggs.
