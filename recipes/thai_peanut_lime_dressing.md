## Ingredients
- 3 tbsp peanut butter
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 2-4 tbsp warm water

## Instructions
1. Whisk peanut butter with warm water.
2. Add lime juice, soy sauce, maple syrup, and ginger.
3. Blend until silky.
4. Adjust sweetness with maple syrup.
5. Use on salads and slaws.

## Notes
Creamy Thai peanut dressing.
