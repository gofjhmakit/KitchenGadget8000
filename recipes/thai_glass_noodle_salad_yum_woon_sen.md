## Ingredients
- 150 g mung bean glass noodles
- 200 g shrimp, poached
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp palm sugar
- Cherry tomatoes
- Fresh mint
- Roasted peanuts
- Shallots

## Instructions
1. Blanch glass noodles; rinse cold.
2. Whisk fish sauce, lime, and palm sugar.
3. Toss noodles with shrimp and tomatoes.
4. Add dressing and mint.
5. Garnish with peanuts and shallots.

## Notes
Tangy Thai glass noodle salad.
