## Ingredients
- 2 cucumbers, diced
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1 red onion, finely diced
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1 tsp chaat masala
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Dice cucumber, tomato, and onion uniformly.
2. Toss with lemon juice and chaat masala.
3. Season with salt.
4. Fold in cilantro.
5. Serve immediately with dal or curry.

## Notes
Fresh Indian chopped salad.
