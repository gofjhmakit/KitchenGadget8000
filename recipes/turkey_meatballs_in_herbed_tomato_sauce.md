## Ingredients
- 500 g turkey mince
- 1 egg
- 40 g breadcrumbs
- 20 g parsley
- 1 garlic clove
- 700 g passata

## Instructions
1. Mix turkey, egg, breadcrumbs, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper.
2. Shape into meatballs and brown in a pan.
3. Add passata and simmer until cooked through.
4. Serve with courgette ribbons or pasta.

## Notes
A good freezer meal.
