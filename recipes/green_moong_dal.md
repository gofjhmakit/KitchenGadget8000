## Ingredients
- 250 g whole green moong beans
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tbsp oil
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Soak green moong 2 hours; simmer with turmeric and salt until tender.
2. Heat oil; toast cumin seeds.
3. Sauté onion until golden; add coriander powder.
4. Combine with dal and simmer 10 minutes.
5. Garnish with cilantro.

## Notes
Whole mung bean dal with more texture.
