## Ingredients
- 2 chicken breasts
- 2 eggs
- 100 g panko breadcrumbs
- 50 g flour
- Shredded cabbage
- Tonkatsu sauce
- Oil for frying

## Instructions
1. Pound chicken thin; season.
2. Bread with flour, egg, and panko.
3. Shallow-fry until golden and cooked through.
4. Slice and serve over cabbage.
5. Drizzle tonkatsu sauce.

## Notes
Crispy Japanese cutlet plate.
