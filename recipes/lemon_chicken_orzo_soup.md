## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 1.2 l chicken stock
- 180 g cooked chicken
- 80 g orzo
- 1 lemon

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion, carrots, and celery in oil.
2. Add stock and bring to a simmer.
3. Cook orzo until tender, then add chicken.
4. Finish with lemon juice, zest, and parsley.

## Notes
A bright weeknight soup.
