## Ingredients
- 400 g wheat noodles
- 3 tbsp Chinese sesame paste
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp chili oil
- 1 cucumber, julienned
- Sliced scallions
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Cook noodles; rinse under cold water and drain.
2. Whisk sesame paste with soy sauce, vinegar, and chili oil.
3. Toss noodles with sauce until coated.
4. Top with cucumber and scallions.
5. Sprinkle sesame seeds before serving.

## Notes
Chilled nutty noodle salad.
