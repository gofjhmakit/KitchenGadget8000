## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 200 g mushrooms, sliced
- 1 garlic clove
- 15 g ginger
- 700 ml water
- 2 tbsp white miso
- 120 g soba noodles
- 2 spring onions

## Instructions
1. Sauté mushrooms, garlic, and ginger in sesame oil.
2. Add water and simmer 5 minutes.
3. Whisk in miso off the heat.
4. Cook noodles separately, divide into bowls, and ladle over broth.

## Notes
Add spinach for extra greens.
