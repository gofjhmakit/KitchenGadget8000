## Ingredients
- 200 g brown rice
- 400 g black beans
- 1 avocado
- 1 corn cob, kernels removed
- 1 lime
- Fresh cilantro
- 1 tsp cumin
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Cook brown rice.
2. Warm beans with cumin and salt.
3. Assemble bowls with rice, beans, corn, and avocado.
4. Finish with lime juice and cilantro.

## Notes
Complete plant-based meal.
