## Ingredients
- 45 g chia seeds
- 350 ml milk of choice
- 2 tbsp cocoa powder
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 50 g dark chocolate, grated

## Instructions
1. Whisk chia, milk, cocoa, and maple syrup.
2. Rest 10 minutes, whisk again, then chill for 4 hours.
3. Top with grated dark chocolate.

## Notes
Add raspberries for contrast.
