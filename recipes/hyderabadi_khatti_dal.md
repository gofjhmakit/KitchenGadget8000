## Ingredients
- 250 g toor dal
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 1 tbsp tamarind paste
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 8 curry leaves
- 2 green chilies
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tbsp oil
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Simmer toor dal with turmeric until soft.
2. Add tomatoes and tamarind; cook 10 minutes.
3. Temper mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chilies in oil.
4. Pour over dal.
5. Adjust sourness and salt.

## Notes
Tangy Hyderabadi tamarind dal.
