## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp sambal oelek
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp palm sugar
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp water
- Minced shallot

## Instructions
1. Dissolve palm sugar in warm water.
2. Whisk in sambal, lime, and soy.
3. Add minced shallot.
4. Rest 10 minutes.
5. Spicy dressing for noodle salads.

## Notes
Fiery Malaysian chili dressing.
