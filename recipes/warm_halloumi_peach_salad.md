## Ingredients
- 180 g halloumi
- 2 ripe peaches
- 80 g mixed leaves
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp honey
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 20 g pistachios

## Instructions
1. Slice peaches and halloumi.
2. Sear halloumi in a dry pan until golden.
3. Whisk oil, honey, and lemon juice.
4. Layer leaves, peaches, halloumi, and pistachios, then dress.

## Notes
Swap peaches for nectarines out of season.
