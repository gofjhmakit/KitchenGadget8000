## Ingredients
- 400 g baby bok choy
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Halve bok choy and steam 5 minutes.
2. Heat oils; fry garlic until fragrant.
3. Add soy sauce off heat.
4. Drizzle garlic soy over bok choy.
5. Serve immediately.

## Notes
Minimal steamed greens.
