## Ingredients
- 400 g chickpeas, drained
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tsp cumin
- 30 g parsley
- 2 tbsp oat flour
- 250 g cooked bulgur
- 120 g yogurt

## Instructions
1. Pulse chickpeas, garlic, cumin, parsley, and oat flour into a coarse mix.
2. Shape into small patties and bake at 210°C until crisp.
3. Serve over bulgur with yogurt herb sauce and salad.
4. Add lemon wedges to finish.

## Notes
Use parchment for easy release.
