## Ingredients
- 200 g split urad dal
- 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tbsp oil
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Simmer urad dal with turmeric until creamy.
2. Heat oil; toast fenugreek seeds briefly.
3. Sauté onion and ginger-garlic paste.
4. Combine with dal and simmer 10 minutes.
5. Garnish with cilantro.

## Notes
Earthy dal with fenugreek notes.
