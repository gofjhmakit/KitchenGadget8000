## Ingredients
- 1 L chicken broth
- 3 eggs, beaten
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp cornstarch slurry
- White pepper
- Scallions
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Bring broth to gentle simmer.
2. Thicken lightly with cornstarch slurry.
3. Stir soup in circular motion; drizzle eggs.
4. Turn off heat; add sesame oil and white pepper.
5. Garnish with scallions.

## Notes
Silky classic egg flower soup.
