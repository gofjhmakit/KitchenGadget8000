## Ingredients
- 700 g carrots, chopped
- 1 onion, diced
- 20 g fresh ginger
- 2 garlic cloves
- 750 ml vegetable stock
- 100 ml coconut milk
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger in oil until fragrant.
2. Add carrots and stock; simmer for 20 minutes until soft.
3. Blend smooth and stir in coconut milk.
4. Season with salt, pepper, and lemon zest.

## Notes
Top with toasted seeds.
