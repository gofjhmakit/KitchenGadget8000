## Ingredients
- 250 g rice noodles
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 2 tbsp red curry paste
- 150 g snow peas
- 100 g red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- Thai basil
- Lime wedges
- 1 tbsp coconut oil

## Instructions
1. Soak rice noodles until tender; drain.
2. Fry curry paste in coconut oil 1 minute.
3. Add coconut milk and soy sauce; simmer 5 minutes.
4. Add vegetables; cook 3 minutes.
5. Toss noodles with curry sauce and basil; serve with lime.

## Notes
Creamy plant-based curry noodles.
