## Ingredients
- 400 g frozen edamame in pods
- 1 tbsp flaky sea salt
- 1 tsp toasted sesame oil

## Instructions
1. Boil edamame in salted water 4 minutes.
2. Drain and toss with sea salt.
3. Drizzle lightly with sesame oil.
4. Serve warm in pods.
5. Sprinkle extra salt at table.

## Notes
Simple Japanese bar snack.
