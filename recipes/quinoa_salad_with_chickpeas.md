## Ingredients
- 200 g quinoa
- 400 g canned chickpeas
- 1 cucumber, diced
- 200 g cherry tomatoes
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Fresh mint
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Cook quinoa and cool.
2. Combine with chickpeas, cucumber, and tomatoes.
3. Dress with olive oil, lemon, and mint.
4. Season and serve chilled.

## Notes
Meal-prep friendly salad.
