## Ingredients
- 150 g mung bean glass noodles
- 200 g firm tofu, cubed
- 150 g bok choy
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp vegetarian oyster sauce
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp white pepper
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Soak glass noodles 10 minutes.
2. Pan-fry tofu until golden.
3. Stir-fry garlic and bok choy 3 minutes.
4. Add noodles, soy sauce, and vegetarian oyster sauce.
5. Toss with tofu and white pepper.

## Notes
Light vegan glass noodle wok.
