## Ingredients
- 300 g daikon radish, julienned
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 3 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- Dried chili threads

## Instructions
1. Salt daikon and carrot; rest 15 minutes; rinse.
2. Dissolve sugar and salt in vinegar.
3. Toss vegetables with vinegar dressing.
4. Chill 30 minutes.
5. Garnish with chili threads.

## Notes
Pickled-style sunomono salad.
