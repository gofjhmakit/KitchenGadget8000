## Ingredients
- 400 g napa cabbage, shredded
- 2 carrots, julienned
- 3 tbsp rice vinegar
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp toasted sesame seeds
- Sliced scallions

## Instructions
1. Shred cabbage and carrot finely.
2. Whisk rice vinegar, sesame oil, and soy sauce.
3. Toss vegetables with dressing.
4. Rest 10 minutes for flavors to meld.
5. Garnish with sesame seeds and scallions.

## Notes
Crunchy sesame cabbage salad.
