## Ingredients
- 24 wonton wrappers
- 200 g ground pork
- 1 tsp ginger
- 1 L chicken broth
- Bok choy
- Soy sauce
- Sesame oil
- Scallions

## Instructions
1. Mix pork with ginger, soy, and sesame oil.
2. Fill wontons and pleat.
3. Simmer broth; add bok choy.
4. Poach wontons in broth 5 minutes.
5. Serve garnished with scallions.

## Notes
Clear broth with pork wontons.
