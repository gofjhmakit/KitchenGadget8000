## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 800 g tomatoes
- 400 g butter beans
- 1 tsp chili flakes
- 20 g basil

## Instructions
1. Cook onion and garlic in olive oil until sweet.
2. Add tomatoes, butter beans, and chili.
3. Simmer 15 minutes until thick.
4. Stir in basil just before serving.

## Notes
Excellent with toasted sourdough.
