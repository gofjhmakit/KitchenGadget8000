## Ingredients
- 250 g rice noodles
- 2 tbsp laksa paste
- 400 ml coconut milk
- 200 g shrimp
- 100 g bean sprouts
- Fish cake slices
- Fresh laksa leaves
- Sambal

## Instructions
1. Fry laksa paste in a little oil 2 minutes.
2. Add coconut milk; simmer 10 minutes.
3. Poach shrimp in broth.
4. Blanch rice noodles.
5. Assemble bowls with broth, noodles, sprouts, fish cake, and laksa leaves.

## Notes
Coconut curry laksa noodle bowl.
