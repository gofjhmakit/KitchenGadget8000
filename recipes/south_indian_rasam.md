## Ingredients
- 1 cup cooked toor dal
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 1 tbsp tamarind paste
- 1 tsp rasam powder
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 8 curry leaves
- 2 dried red chilies
- Fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Simmer tomatoes and tamarind in 500 ml water 10 minutes.
2. Add cooked dal and rasam powder.
3. Temper mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chilies in oil.
4. Pour tempering into rasam.
5. Garnish with cilantro.

## Notes
Tangy South Indian lentil soup.
