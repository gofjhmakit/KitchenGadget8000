## Ingredients
- 400 g green beans
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves
- 30 g flaked almonds
- 1 tsp lemon zest

## Instructions
1. Blanch beans until crisp-tender.
2. Sauté garlic in olive oil and add almonds.
3. Toss beans in the pan and finish with lemon zest.

## Notes
A classic side for fish or chicken.
