## Ingredients
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 3 tbsp lime juice
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 bird's eye chili, sliced
- 2 tbsp warm water

## Instructions
1. Dissolve sugar in warm water.
2. Whisk in fish sauce and lime juice.
3. Add garlic and chili.
4. Taste and adjust sweet-sour-salty balance.
5. Store refrigerated 2 weeks.

## Notes
Essential Vietnamese dipping dressing.
