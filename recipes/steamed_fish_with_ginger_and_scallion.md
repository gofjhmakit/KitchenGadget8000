## Ingredients
- 400 g whole sea bass or fillets
- 3 cm ginger, julienned
- 4 scallions, julienned
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp shaoxing wine
- 2 tbsp hot oil

## Instructions
1. Score fish and place on steaming plate.
2. Top with ginger and half the scallions.
3. Steam 10-12 minutes until flaky.
4. Drizzle soy and wine over.
5. Top with remaining scallions and pour hot oil.

## Notes
Cantonese classic steamed fish.
