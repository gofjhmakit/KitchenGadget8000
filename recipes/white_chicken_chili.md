## Ingredients
- 2 cans great northern white beans, drained and rinsed
- 2 cans diced green chiles
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Sour cream, shredded cheese, tortilla chips for topping
- 1, shredded rotisserie chicken
- 4 cups chicken broth
- 8 oz block cream cheese

## Instructions
1. Add shredded rotisserie chicken, white beans, green chiles, onion, garlic, cumin, oregano, cayenne, and chicken broth to the slow cooker. Stir.
2. Cover and cook on LOW for 4 hours or HIGH for 2 hours.
3. Cut the cream cheese into cubes and stir into the chili during the last 30 minutes of cooking. Stir occasionally until fully melted and creamy.
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
5. Serve topped with sour cream, shredded cheese, and crushed tortilla chips.

## Notes
Creamy, slightly spicy, and suspiciously easy for something this comforting.
