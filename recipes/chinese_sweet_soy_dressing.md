## Ingredients
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp sugar
- Grated ginger (1 tsp)

## Instructions
1. Whisk all ingredients until smooth.
2. Warm gently to dissolve sugar.
3. Cool before use.
4. Drizzle on cold noodles or salads.
5. Store refrigerated 1 week.

## Notes
Sweet savory Chinese dressing.
