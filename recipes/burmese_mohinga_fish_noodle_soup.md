## Ingredients
- 300 g catfish fillet
- 200 g rice noodles
- 2 tbsp rice flour
- 1 tbsp turmeric
- Lemongrass
- Banana stem or cabbage
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Crispy chickpea fritters
- Fish sauce
- Lime

## Instructions
1. Simmer fish with lemongrass and turmeric; flake meat.
2. Thicken broth with rice flour slurry.
3. Blanch noodles.
4. Assemble with fish broth, banana stem, and egg.
5. Top with fritters, fish sauce, and lime.

## Notes
National dish of Myanmar.
