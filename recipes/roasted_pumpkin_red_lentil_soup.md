## Ingredients
- 700 g pumpkin, cubed
- 150 g red lentils
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 750 ml vegetable stock
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Roast pumpkin with oil at 210°C until caramelized.
2. Cook onion and garlic until soft.
3. Add lentils, stock, cumin, and roasted pumpkin.
4. Simmer until lentils break down, then blend.

## Notes
Add chili flakes for warmth.
