## Ingredients
- 50 g each: moong, masoor, chana, toor, urad dal
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tbsp ghee
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Rinse all dals; simmer together with turmeric and salt until creamy.
2. Heat ghee; toast cumin seeds.
3. Sauté onion until golden; add tomatoes and spices.
4. Cook masala 8 minutes.
5. Combine with dal, simmer 10 minutes, garnish with cilantro.

## Notes
Rajasthani five-lentil blend.
