## Ingredients
- 200 g horse gram (kulthi dal)
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 8 curry leaves
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- Salt
- Fresh curry leaves

## Instructions
1. Soak horse gram 4 hours; pressure-cook or simmer until tender.
2. Heat coconut oil; pop mustard seeds and curry leaves.
3. Sauté onion and garlic until soft.
4. Add cooked dal and turmeric; simmer 15 minutes.
5. Season and serve with rice.

## Notes
Protein-dense South Indian dal.
