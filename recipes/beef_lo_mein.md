## Ingredients
- 400 g lo mein noodles
- 250 g flank steak, sliced thin
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 150 g snow peas
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Toss beef with cornstarch.
2. Cook noodles; drain.
3. Stir-fry beef in hot oil until browned; remove.
4. Stir-fry pepper, snow peas, and garlic.
5. Return beef with noodles, soy sauce, and hoisin; toss 2 minutes.

## Notes
Savory beef tossed noodles.
