## Ingredients
- 300 g rice vermicelli
- 200 g shrimp
- 150 g chicken breast, sliced
- 2 tbsp curry powder
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Lime wedges
- Fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Soak vermicelli until soft; drain.
2. Stir-fry chicken and shrimp in oil until cooked.
3. Add curry powder and bell pepper; cook 2 minutes.
4. Add coconut milk and fish sauce; simmer 5 minutes.
5. Toss in noodles, garnish with cilantro and lime.

## Notes
Curry-spiced rice noodle stir fry.
