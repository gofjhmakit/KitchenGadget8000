## Ingredients
- 800 g chicken thighs
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 8 garlic cloves
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tsp black peppercorns
- Steamed rice

## Instructions
1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
2. Simmer covered 30 minutes.
3. Uncover and reduce sauce until glossy.
4. Serve over steamed rice.
5. Spoon pan sauce over chicken.

## Notes
Tangy soy-vinegar braised chicken.
