## Ingredients
- 2 Asian eggplants
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1 red onion, sliced
- 3 tbsp coconut vinegar
- 1 tsp salt
- Bird's eye chili, minced

## Instructions
1. Grill eggplants until charred; peel and mash lightly.
2. Combine tomatoes and onion.
3. Toss with vinegar, salt, and chili.
4. Mix in eggplant.
5. Serve at room temperature.

## Notes
Filipino grilled eggplant salad.
