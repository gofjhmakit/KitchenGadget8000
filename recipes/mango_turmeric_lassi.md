## Ingredients
- 250 g mango
- 200 g yogurt
- 120 ml milk
- 1 pinch turmeric
- 1 tsp honey
- 2 ice cubes

## Instructions
1. Blend mango, yogurt, milk, turmeric, honey, and ice until frothy.
2. Pour into cold glasses.

## Notes
A sunny afternoon drink.
