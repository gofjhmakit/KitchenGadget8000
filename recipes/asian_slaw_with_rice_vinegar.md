## Ingredients
- 300 g red cabbage, shredded
- 2 carrots, grated
- 1 apple, julienned
- 3 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp neutral oil
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 1 tsp honey
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Combine cabbage, carrot, and apple.
2. Whisk vinegar, oil, ginger, honey, and salt.
3. Massage slaw with dressing 1 minute.
4. Rest 15 minutes.
5. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

## Notes
Meal-prep friendly crunchy slaw.
