## Ingredients
- 200 g moong dal
- 300 g bottle gourd (lauki), peeled and cubed
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 green chili, slit
- 2 tbsp oil
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Simmer moong dal with bottle gourd, turmeric, and salt until both are tender.
2. Heat oil; toast cumin and green chili.
3. Pour tempering over dal.
4. Simmer 5 more minutes.
5. Garnish with cilantro.

## Notes
Light digestible summer dal.
