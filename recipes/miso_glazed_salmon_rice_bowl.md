## Ingredients
- 4 salmon portions
- 2 tbsp white miso
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp honey
- 300 g cooked brown rice
- 1 cucumber
- 1 carrot
- 1 avocado

## Instructions
1. Whisk miso, soy, and honey.
2. Brush over salmon and bake at 210°C for 12 minutes.
3. Divide rice into bowls and add vegetables.
4. Top with salmon and any pan glaze.

## Notes
Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
