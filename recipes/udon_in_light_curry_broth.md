## Ingredients
- 400 g frozen udon
- 1 tbsp Japanese curry roux
- 500 ml dashi
- 100 g sliced onion
- Fish cake slices
- Soft-boiled egg
- Sliced scallions

## Instructions
1. Simmer onion in dashi 5 minutes.
2. Dissolve curry roux into broth.
3. Blanch udon; place in bowls.
4. Ladle curry broth over noodles.
5. Top with fish cake, egg, and scallions.

## Notes
Comfort Japanese curry udon.
