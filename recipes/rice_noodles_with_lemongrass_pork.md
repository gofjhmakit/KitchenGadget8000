## Ingredients
- 300 g rice vermicelli
- 300 g pork shoulder, sliced
- 2 stalks lemongrass, minced
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Lettuce leaves
- Fresh mint
- Crushed peanuts
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Marinate pork with lemongrass, fish sauce, and sugar 20 minutes.
2. Grill or stir-fry pork until charred.
3. Soak vermicelli in hot water; drain.
4. Serve noodles with pork, lettuce, and herbs.
5. Sprinkle peanuts on top.
