## Ingredients
- 400 g canned chickpeas, rinsed
- 200 g cherry tomatoes
- 1 cucumber
- 80 g olives
- 40 g red onion
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tsp dried oregano

## Instructions
1. Halve tomatoes and dice cucumber.
2. Mix chickpeas, tomatoes, cucumber, olives, and onion.
3. Dress with olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
4. Rest 10 minutes before serving.

## Notes
Fold in herbs like mint or parsley.
