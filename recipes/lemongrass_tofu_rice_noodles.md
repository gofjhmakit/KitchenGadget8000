## Ingredients
- 250 g rice noodles
- 300 g firm tofu, cubed
- 2 stalks lemongrass, minced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- Bean sprouts
- Fresh cilantro
- Crushed peanuts
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Pan-fry tofu until golden.
2. Stir-fry lemongrass; add soy sauce and maple syrup.
3. Coat tofu in lemongrass glaze.
4. Soak rice noodles; drain.
5. Serve with sprouts, cilantro, and peanuts.

## Notes
Fragrant vegan rice noodle bowl.
