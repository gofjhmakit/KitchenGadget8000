## Ingredients
- 300 g all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp yeast
- 200 g bok choy, chopped
- 100 g shiitake mushrooms
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp sugar

## Instructions
1. Make soft yeast dough; rise 1 hour.
2. Stir-fry bok choy and mushrooms with soy and sesame oil.
3. Divide dough; fill with vegetable mixture.
4. Steam bao 12 minutes.
5. Serve warm.

## Notes
Fluffy steamed vegetable buns.
