## Ingredients
- 400 g firm tofu, sliced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp gochugaru
- Scallions
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Pat tofu dry; pan-fry until golden both sides.
2. Mix soy sauce, sesame oil, and gochugaru.
3. Brush sauce on tofu slices.
4. Simmer 2 minutes until glazed.
5. Garnish with scallions and sesame seeds.

## Notes
Spicy glazed pan-fried tofu.
