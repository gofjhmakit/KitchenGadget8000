## Ingredients
- 30 g dried wakame seaweed
- 2 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- Grated ginger (1 tsp)

## Instructions
1. Soak wakame in water 5 minutes; drain and squeeze.
2. Grind half the sesame seeds.
3. Whisk soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, and ginger.
4. Toss wakame with dressing and ground sesame.
5. Top with whole sesame seeds.

## Notes
Classic seaweed sesame salad.
