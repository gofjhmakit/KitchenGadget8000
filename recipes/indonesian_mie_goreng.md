## Ingredients
- 300 g egg noodles
- 2 eggs
- 150 g chicken breast, diced
- 2 tbsp kecap manis
- 1 tbsp sambal oelek
- 100 g cabbage, shredded
- 2 garlic cloves
- Fried shallots
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Boil noodles; drain well.
2. Scramble eggs in wok; set aside.
3. Stir-fry chicken and garlic until cooked.
4. Add cabbage, noodles, kecap manis, and sambal; toss 3 minutes.
5. Top with scrambled egg and fried shallots.

## Notes
Sweet-spicy Indonesian fried noodles.
