## Ingredients
- 250 g ricotta
- 1 lemon, zested
- 1 tbsp honey
- 80 g blueberries
- 20 g toasted almonds

## Instructions
1. Whisk ricotta with lemon zest and honey until smooth.
2. Spoon into small glasses.
3. Top with blueberries and almonds.

## Notes
Elegant enough for guests.
