## Ingredients
- 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
- Fresh basil, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 4 slices crusty bread
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Combine tomatoes, basil, garlic, oil, vinegar, and salt.
2. Rest 10 minutes.
3. Toast bread.
4. Top bread with tomato mixture and serve.

## Notes
Classic Italian appetizer.
