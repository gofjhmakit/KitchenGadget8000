## Ingredients
- 200 g toor dal
- 100 g whole wheat flour
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 8 curry leaves
- 2 tbsp oil
- Tamarind (1 tbsp paste)
- Salt
- Fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Simmer toor dal with turmeric until soft; add tamarind.
2. Knead flour with water and pinch of salt into stiff dough; roll and cut into diamonds.
3. Drop dhokli into simmering dal; cook 12 minutes.
4. Temper mustard seeds and curry leaves in oil.
5. Pour over dal, garnish with cilantro.

## Notes
Gujarati wheat dumplings in lentil broth.
