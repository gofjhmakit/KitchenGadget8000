## Ingredients
- 1 kg beef bones
- 300 g beef sirloin, sliced thin
- 200 g rice noodles
- 1 onion, charred
- 3 star anise
- 1 cinnamon stick
- Fresh basil and bean sprouts
- Fish sauce
- Lime

## Instructions
1. Simmer bones with charred onion and spices 2 hours.
2. Strain broth; season with fish sauce.
3. Blanch rice noodles.
4. Top with raw beef slices; ladle hot broth.
5. Serve with herbs, sprouts, and lime.

## Notes
Classic Hanoi beef noodle soup.
