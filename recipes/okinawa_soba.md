## Ingredients
- 400 g Okinawa soba noodles
- 200 g pork belly, sliced
- 1 L dashi stock
- 2 tbsp awamori or sake
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- Pickled ginger
- Fish cake slices
- Sliced scallions

## Instructions
1. Simmer pork belly in dashi with awamori 20 minutes.
2. Season broth with soy sauce.
3. Cook Okinawa soba separately; divide into bowls.
4. Ladle hot broth and pork over noodles.
5. Top with fish cake, scallions, and pickled ginger.

## Notes
Hearty Okinawan wheat noodle soup bowl.
