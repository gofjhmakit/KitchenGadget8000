## Ingredients
- 220 g rolled oats
- 2 pears, diced
- 40 g chopped hazelnuts
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 500 ml milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp maple syrup

## Instructions
1. Mix oats, pears, hazelnuts, and cinnamon in a baking dish.
2. Whisk milk, egg, and maple syrup and pour over.
3. Bake at 190°C for 25 minutes until set.

## Notes
Serve with yogurt.
