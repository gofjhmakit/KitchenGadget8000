## Ingredients
- 400 g hakka noodles
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 150 g cabbage, shredded
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Sliced scallions
- White pepper

## Instructions
1. Boil hakka noodles; rinse cold.
2. Stir-fry vegetables in oil over high heat.
3. Add noodles, soy sauce, and vinegar.
4. Toss 3 minutes until smoky.
5. Season with white pepper and scallions.

## Notes
Indo-Chinese stir-fried noodles.
