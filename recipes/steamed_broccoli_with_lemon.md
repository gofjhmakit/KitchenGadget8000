## Ingredients
- 500 g broccoli florets
- 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- Chili flakes (optional)

## Instructions
1. Steam broccoli 5-6 minutes until bright green and tender.
2. Toss with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
3. Serve immediately.

## Notes
Simple everyday vegetable side.
