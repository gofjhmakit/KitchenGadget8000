## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp white miso
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 1 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp water

## Instructions
1. Blend miso with vinegar and water.
2. Whisk in sesame oil, ginger, and honey.
3. Adjust thickness with water.
4. Refrigerate until use.
5. Great on green salads and noodles.

## Notes
Savory miso salad dressing.
