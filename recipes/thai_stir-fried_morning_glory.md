## Ingredients
- 400 g morning glory (water spinach)
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce or vegetarian oyster sauce
- 3 garlic cloves
- 2 bird's eye chilies
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Cut morning glory into 8 cm lengths.
2. Stir-fry garlic and chilies in hot oil.
3. Add morning glory; toss over high heat 2 minutes.
4. Add oyster sauce and splash of water.
5. Serve immediately while crisp-tender.

## Notes
Classic pad pak boong.
