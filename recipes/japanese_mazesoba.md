## Ingredients
- 200 g fresh ramen noodles
- 150 g ground pork
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp mirin
- Soft-boiled egg
- Menma bamboo shoots
- Nori
- Sliced scallions
- Sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Cook ramen; drain well.
2. Brown pork with soy sauce and mirin.
3. Place noodles in bowl with minimal tare sauce.
4. Top with pork, egg, menma, nori, and scallions.
5. Toss at the table before eating.

## Notes
Brothless Japanese mixed noodles.
