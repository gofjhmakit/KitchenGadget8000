## Ingredients
- 400 g chicken thigh, sliced
- 400 ml coconut milk
- 500 ml chicken broth
- 2 stalks lemongrass
- 4 kaffir lime leaves
- 100 g mushrooms
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- Bird's eye chilies

## Instructions
1. Simmer broth with lemongrass and lime leaves 8 minutes.
2. Add chicken and mushrooms; cook 5 minutes.
3. Stir in coconut milk; do not boil hard.
4. Season with fish sauce and lime.
5. Serve with chilies.

## Notes
Creamy fragrant Thai coconut soup.
