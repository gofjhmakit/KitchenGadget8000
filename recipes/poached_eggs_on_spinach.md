## Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- 200 g spinach
- 1 tbsp white vinegar
- 2 slices whole grain toast
- Salt and pepper
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Wilt spinach in olive oil; keep warm.
2. Simmer water with vinegar; poach eggs 3 minutes.
3. Toast bread.
4. Serve eggs over spinach with toast.

## Notes
Light protein-rich breakfast.
