## Ingredients
- 400 g Korean radish (mu), julienned
- 2 tbsp gochugaru
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- Garlic, minced
- Scallions

## Instructions
1. Julienne radish into matchsticks.
2. Massage with salt 10 minutes; drain.
3. Mix gochugaru, vinegar, sugar, fish sauce, and garlic.
4. Toss radish with spicy dressing.
5. Garnish with scallions; serve chilled.

## Notes
Spicy crunchy Korean radish salad.
