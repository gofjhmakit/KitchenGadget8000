## Ingredients
- 500 g Swiss chard or kale
- 3 garlic cloves, sliced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Red pepper flakes
- Lemon juice
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Wash greens and remove tough stems.
2. Sauté garlic in oil until fragrant.
3. Add greens in batches until wilted.
4. Season with salt, pepper flakes, and lemon.

## Notes
Simple side for any meal.
