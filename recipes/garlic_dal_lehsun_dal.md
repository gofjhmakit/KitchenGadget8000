## Ingredients
- 250 g moong dal
- 6 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 2 dried red chilies
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tbsp ghee
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Simmer moong dal with turmeric until creamy.
2. Heat ghee; add cumin, chilies, and garlic.
3. Cook garlic until fragrant, not burnt.
4. Pour over dal and simmer 5 minutes.
5. Serve hot with rice or roti.

## Notes
Pungent garlic tempered dal.
