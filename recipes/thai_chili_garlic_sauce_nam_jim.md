## Ingredients
- 4 tbsp rice vinegar
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 2 tbsp chili flakes
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp warm water

## Instructions
1. Dissolve sugar in warm water.
2. Stir in vinegar, chili, garlic, and soy.
3. Rest 15 minutes for flavors to bloom.
4. Adjust heat with more chili if needed.
5. Refrigerate up to 2 weeks.

## Notes
Versatile Thai chili dipping sauce.
