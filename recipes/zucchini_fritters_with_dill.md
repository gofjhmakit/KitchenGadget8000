## Ingredients
- 2 courgettes
- 1 egg
- 40 g flour
- 30 g feta
- 1 tbsp dill
- olive oil for frying

## Instructions
1. Grate courgettes, salt lightly, and squeeze out excess moisture.
2. Mix with egg, flour, feta, and dill.
3. Pan-fry spoonfuls in a little oil until golden.
4. Serve warm.

## Notes
Lovely with yogurt and lemon.
