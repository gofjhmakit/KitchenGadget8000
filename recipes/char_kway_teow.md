## Ingredients
- 200 g flat rice noodles
- 150 g shrimp
- 2 eggs
- 100 g bean sprouts
- 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 garlic cloves
- Chinese chives
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Stir-fry garlic and shrimp in hot wok.
2. Push aside; scramble eggs.
3. Add noodles, dark soy, and fish sauce; toss over high heat.
4. Fold in bean sprouts and chives.
5. Serve immediately from the wok.

## Notes
Malaysian wok-charred flat noodles.
