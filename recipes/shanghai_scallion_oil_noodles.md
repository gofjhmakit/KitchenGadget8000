## Ingredients
- 300 g fresh Shanghai noodles
- 6 scallions, sliced
- 4 tbsp neutral oil
- 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tsp sugar

## Instructions
1. Cook noodles until al dente; drain.
2. Slowly fry scallions in oil until deeply golden.
3. Mix dark soy, light soy, and sugar into scallion oil.
4. Toss hot noodles with sauce until coated.
5. Serve immediately while glossy.

## Notes
Simple four-ingredient Shanghai classic.
