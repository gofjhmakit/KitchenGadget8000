## Ingredients
- 250 g toor dal
- 8 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 2 dried red chilies
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 3 tbsp oil
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Cook toor dal with turmeric until soft and creamy.
2. Fry garlic slices in oil until deep golden and crisp.
3. Remove half the garlic; add cumin and chilies to oil.
4. Pour tempering over dal.
5. Top with remaining crispy garlic and cilantro.

## Notes
Smoky garlic-forward dal.
