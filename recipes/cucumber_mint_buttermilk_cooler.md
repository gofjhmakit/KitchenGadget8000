## Ingredients
- 200 ml buttermilk
- 80 g cucumber
- 8 mint leaves
- 1 pinch cumin
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions
1. Blend everything until pale green and foamy.
2. Pour over ice if desired.

## Notes
Inspired by salty summer coolers.
