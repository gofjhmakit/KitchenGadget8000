## Ingredients
- 300 g tempeh, cubed
- 3 tbsp kecap manis
- 2 tbsp sambal oelek
- 2 garlic cloves
- Kaffir lime leaves
- Sliced shallots
- 2 tbsp oil

## Instructions
1. Pan-fry tempeh until golden and crisp.
2. Sauté shallots, garlic, and lime leaves.
3. Add kecap manis and sambal.
4. Toss tempeh in glaze until sticky.
5. Serve with rice.

## Notes
Sweet-spicy Indonesian tempeh.
