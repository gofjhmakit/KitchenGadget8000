## Ingredients
- 150 g chana dal
- 100 g toor dal
- 50 g moong dal
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 2 tbsp ghee
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Simmer all dals with salt until thick and creamy.
2. Heat ghee; sauté onion until golden.
3. Add tomatoes and spices; cook 8 minutes.
4. Combine with dal and simmer 12 minutes.
5. Finish with cilantro.

## Notes
Rajasthani mixed dal traditionally served with baati.
