## Ingredients
- 300 g rice bihon noodles
- 200 g chicken breast, sliced
- 1 cup shredded cabbage
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp calamansi juice
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Soak bihon until soft.
2. Stir-fry chicken and garlic.
3. Add vegetables; cook 3 minutes.
4. Toss noodles with soy sauce and splash of water.
5. Finish with calamansi juice.

## Notes
Filipino stir-fried rice noodles.
