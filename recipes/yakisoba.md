## Ingredients
- 300 g yakisoba noodles
- 150 g sliced pork belly
- 1 cup shredded cabbage
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 3 tbsp yakisoba sauce
- Pickled ginger
- Aonori seaweed
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Stir-fry pork in oil until browned.
2. Add cabbage and carrot; cook 3 minutes.
3. Add noodles and yakisoba sauce; toss 4 minutes.
4. Serve with pickled ginger.
5. Sprinkle aonori on top.

## Notes
Japanese festival-style fried noodles.
