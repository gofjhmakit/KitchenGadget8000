## Ingredients
- 30 gyoza wrappers
- 200 g ground pork
- 150 g napa cabbage, minced
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 120 ml water

## Instructions
1. Mix pork, cabbage, soy, ginger, and garlic.
2. Fill wrappers and pleat edges.
3. Pan-fry in oil until golden on bottom.
4. Add water and cover; steam 5 minutes.
5. Uncover and crisp bottoms again.

## Notes
Crispy-bottomed Japanese dumplings.
