## Ingredients
- 400 g chicken thigh, boneless
- 2 tbsp gochujang
- 1 tbsp gochugaru
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Cabbage, sliced
- Sweet potato, sliced
- Rice cakes
- Perilla leaves

## Instructions
1. Marinate chicken with gochujang, gochugaru, soy, and sugar.
2. Stir-fry chicken over high heat.
3. Add cabbage, sweet potato, and rice cakes.
4. Cook until vegetables tender.
5. Serve wrapped in perilla leaves.

## Notes
Spicy stir-fried chicken.
