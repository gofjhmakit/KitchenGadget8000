## Ingredients
- 1 English cucumber, chopped into chunks
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- Salt and pepper to taste
- >
- 1 block ) feta cheese block
- 4–5 roma tomatoes

## Instructions
1. Chop the tomatoes and cucumber into generous bite-sized chunks. Nobody wants dainty pieces here — think rustic, not restaurant.
2. Thinly slice the red onion. If raw onion scares you, soak the slices in cold water for 5 minutes to mellow them out.
3. Combine the tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion in a large bowl.
4. Scatter the Kalamata olives over the top.
5. Crumble the feta generously over everything. Don't be shy — this is the main event.
6. Whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
7. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently.
