## Ingredients
- 3 Japanese cucumbers, thinly sliced
- 3 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp dried wakame
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Salt cucumbers; rest 10 minutes; squeeze dry.
2. Rehydrate wakame in warm water.
3. Dissolve sugar and salt in rice vinegar.
4. Toss cucumbers and wakame with vinegar.
5. Chill and garnish with sesame seeds.

## Notes
Light sweet vinegar cucumber salad.
