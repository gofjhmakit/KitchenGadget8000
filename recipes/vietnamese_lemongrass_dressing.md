## Ingredients
- 1 stalk lemongrass, minced
- 3 tbsp rice vinegar
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp neutral oil
- 1 garlic clove

## Instructions
1. Mince tender inner lemongrass finely.
2. Whisk with vinegar, soy, maple syrup, and oil.
3. Add minced garlic.
4. Steep 15 minutes.
5. Strain if desired before dressing salads.

## Notes
Fragrant lemongrass vinaigrette.
