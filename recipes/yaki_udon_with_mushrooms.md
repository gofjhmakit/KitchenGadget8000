## Ingredients
- 400 g frozen udon noodles
- 200 g shiitake mushrooms, sliced
- 100 g cabbage, shredded
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 sheet nori, shredded
- Pickled ginger

## Instructions
1. Blanch udon in boiling water 2 minutes; drain.
2. Stir-fry mushrooms and cabbage over high heat 4 minutes.
3. Add udon, soy sauce, and mirin; toss 3 minutes.
4. Drizzle sesame oil and top with nori.
5. Serve with pickled ginger.

## Notes
Quick Japanese stir-fried thick noodles.
