## Ingredients
- 300 g rice vermicelli
- 200 g firm tofu, cubed
- 3 tbsp tamarind paste
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- Bean sprouts
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Fried shallots
- Sambal

## Instructions
1. Soak vermicelli; drain.
2. Simmer tamarind, sugar, and soy sauce into a tangy gravy.
3. Pan-fry tofu cubes.
4. Toss vermicelli with gravy and tofu.
5. Serve with egg, sprouts, shallots, and sambal.

## Notes
Nyonya sweet-sour rice noodles.
