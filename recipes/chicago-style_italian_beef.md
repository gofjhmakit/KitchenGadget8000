## Ingredients
- 1 packet Italian dressing mix
- 1 jar Giardiniera, hot or mild, 16 oz — this is non-negotiable
- 4-5 Pepperoncini peppers + 1/4 cup of their jar juice
- 8 Hoagie rolls
- 3 lbs usda choice beef chuck roast
- 2 cups chicken broth

## Instructions
1. Place the chuck roast in the slow cooker fat-side up.
2. Sprinkle the Italian dressing mix all over the top and sides of the roast.
3. Tuck the pepperoncini peppers around the roast.
4. Cover and cook on LOW for 8 hours.
5. The meat should shred with zero resistance.
6. Pull the beef out onto a cutting board.
7. Return the shredded beef to the pot.
8. Toast the hoagie rolls under the broiler until just golden, about 2 minutes.
9. Pile the beef generously onto the rolls.
10. Serve each sandwich with a small bowl of warm jus for dipping.
