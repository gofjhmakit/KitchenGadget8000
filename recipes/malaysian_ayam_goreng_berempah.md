## Ingredients
- 800 g chicken pieces
- 2 tbsp turmeric
- 1 tbsp coriander powder
- 3 garlic cloves
- 2 cm ginger
- 1 lemongrass stalk
- Coconut milk (100 ml)
- Salt
- Fried shallots

## Instructions
1. Blend spices, garlic, ginger, and lemongrass into paste.
2. Marinate chicken 1 hour.
3. Simmer chicken in coconut milk until tender.
4. Pan-fry or bake until skin crisps.
5. Garnish with fried shallots.

## Notes
Spiced Malaysian fried chicken.
