## Ingredients
- 400 g spaghetti
- 6 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- 1 tsp red pepper flakes
- 80 ml extra virgin olive oil
- Fresh parsley
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente; reserve 120 ml pasta water.
2. Gently sauté garlic and pepper flakes in olive oil until fragrant, not brown.
3. Toss drained pasta with garlic oil and a splash of pasta water.
4. Finish with parsley and serve immediately.

## Notes
Classic Roman pantry pasta.
