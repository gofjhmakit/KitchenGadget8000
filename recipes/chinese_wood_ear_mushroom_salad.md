## Ingredients
- 50 g dried wood ear mushrooms
- 1 cucumber, julienned
- 2 tbsp black vinegar
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp chili oil
- Garlic, minced
- Cilantro

## Instructions
1. Rehydrate wood ear; blanch 2 minutes; slice.
2. Toss with cucumber.
3. Whisk vinegar, soy, and chili oil.
4. Add garlic and dressing.
5. Garnish with cilantro.

## Notes
Crunchy Sichuan mushroom salad.
