## Ingredients
- 3 tbsp Chinkiang black vinegar
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- Sliced scallions

## Instructions
1. Whisk black vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, and sugar.
2. Stir in garlic and scallions.
3. Use immediately on dumplings or salads.
4. Adjust sweetness to taste.
5. Best used fresh.

## Notes
Tangy dumpling dipping sauce.
