## Ingredients
- 300 g thin wheat noodles
- 6 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp chili flakes
- 3 tbsp neutral oil
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp black vinegar
- Sliced scallions
- Cilantro

## Instructions
1. Cook noodles; drain, reserving 2 tbsp water.
2. Heat oil; fry garlic and chili flakes until fragrant.
3. Mix soy sauce, vinegar, and noodle water.
4. Toss noodles with chili oil and sauce.
5. Garnish with scallions and cilantro.

## Notes
Biang biang-inspired chili oil noodles.
