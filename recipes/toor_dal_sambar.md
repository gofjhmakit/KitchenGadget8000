## Ingredients
- 200 g toor dal (pigeon peas)
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (drumstick, carrot, pumpkin)
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 2 tbsp sambar powder
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 8 curry leaves
- 1 dried red chili
- 2 tbsp oil
- Tamarind paste (1 tbsp)
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Cook toor dal until soft; mash lightly.
2. Simmer vegetables in water with sambar powder and tamarind until tender.
3. Add cooked dal and simmer 10 minutes.
4. Temper mustard seeds, curry leaves, and dried chili in oil.
5. Pour tempering over sambar and serve with rice.

## Notes
South Indian lentil-vegetable stew.
