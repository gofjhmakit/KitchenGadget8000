## Ingredients
- 2 corn cobs, kernels removed
- 200 g cherry tomatoes, halved
- Fresh Thai basil
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp palm sugar
- Shallots, sliced

## Instructions
1. Char corn kernels in dry pan 3 minutes.
2. Combine with tomatoes and shallots.
3. Whisk lime, soy, and palm sugar.
4. Toss with Thai basil.
5. Serve warm or room temperature.

## Notes
Smoky corn tomato Thai salad.
