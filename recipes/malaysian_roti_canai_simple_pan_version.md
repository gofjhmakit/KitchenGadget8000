## Ingredients
- 300 g all-purpose flour
- 180 ml warm water
- 2 tbsp ghee
- 1 tsp salt
- Oil for pan frying

## Instructions
1. Knead flour, water, salt, and 1 tbsp ghee into smooth dough.
2. Rest 30 minutes.
3. Divide and stretch into thin rounds.
4. Fold into squares.
5. Pan-fry in ghee until golden and flaky.

## Notes
Flaky Malaysian flatbread.
