## Ingredients
- 400 g chicken thigh, cubed
- 100 g roasted cashews
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 3 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- Dried chilies
- Garlic
- Scallions

## Instructions
1. Stir-fry chicken until golden; set aside.
2. Fry dried chilies and garlic.
3. Return chicken with bell pepper and sauces.
4. Toss in cashews off heat.
5. Garnish with scallions.

## Notes
Sweet-savory Thai cashew stir fry.
