## Ingredients
- 250 g split moong dal
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 2 cm ginger, grated
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 2 tbsp ghee or oil
- 800 ml water
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Rinse moong dal and simmer with turmeric, salt, and water until soft, about 25 minutes.
2. Heat ghee; add cumin seeds until they pop.
3. Sauté onion, ginger, and garlic until golden.
4. Add tomatoes and garam masala; cook 5 minutes.
5. Stir tempering into dal, simmer 5 minutes, garnish with cilantro.

## Notes
Light everyday yellow lentil dal.
