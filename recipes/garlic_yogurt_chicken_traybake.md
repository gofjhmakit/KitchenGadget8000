## Ingredients
- 700 g chicken thighs
- 150 g Greek yogurt
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp cumin
- 500 g baby potatoes
- 1 red onion
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Mix yogurt, garlic, cumin, salt, and pepper.
2. Coat chicken and marinate while the oven heats.
3. Roast chicken, potatoes, and onion at 210°C for 35 minutes.
4. Rest 5 minutes before serving.

## Notes
Add a cucumber salad alongside.
