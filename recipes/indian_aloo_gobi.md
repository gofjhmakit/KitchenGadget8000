## Ingredients
- 2 potatoes, cubed
- 1 cauliflower, florets
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 2 tbsp oil
- Fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Par-cook potatoes in salted water.
2. Toast cumin seeds in oil; add turmeric.
3. Add cauliflower and potatoes; cook 10 minutes.
4. Add tomatoes and coriander powder; simmer 8 minutes.
5. Garnish with cilantro.

## Notes
Dry potato cauliflower curry.
