## Ingredients
- 400 g Chinese broccoli (gai lan)
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tbsp water

## Instructions
1. Blanch gai lan in boiling water 2 minutes; drain.
2. Heat oil; sauté garlic 30 seconds.
3. Add oyster sauce, sugar, and water.
4. Simmer sauce 1 minute.
5. Pour over gai lan and serve.

## Notes
Classic Cantonese greens.
