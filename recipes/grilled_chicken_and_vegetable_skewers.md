## Ingredients
- 500 g chicken breast, cubed
- 2 peppers, chunked
- 1 courgette, sliced
- 200 g cherry tomatoes
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp oregano
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Marinate chicken and vegetables with oil and spices.
2. Thread onto skewers.
3. Grill 10-12 minutes, turning, until chicken is cooked.
4. Serve with lemon wedges.

## Notes
Great for summer cooking.
