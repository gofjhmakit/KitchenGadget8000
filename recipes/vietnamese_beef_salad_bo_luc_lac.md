## Ingredients
- 300 g sirloin steak, cubed
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- Lettuce, tomato, and cucumber
- Lime-pepper dipping salt
- Sliced onion

## Instructions
1. Marinate beef with soy, fish sauce, and oyster sauce.
2. Sear beef cubes over high heat 2 minutes.
3. Arrange lettuce, tomato, and cucumber.
4. Top with beef and onion.
5. Serve with lime-pepper salt.

## Notes
