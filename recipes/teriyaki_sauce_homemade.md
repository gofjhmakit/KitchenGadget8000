## Ingredients
- 4 tbsp soy sauce
- 4 tbsp mirin
- 2 tbsp sake
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 1 garlic clove, minced

## Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in saucepan.
2. Simmer over medium heat 8 minutes until glossy.
3. Cool completely.
4. Store in jar refrigerated.
5. Use for grilling or stir fries.

## Notes
Classic sweet soy glaze.
