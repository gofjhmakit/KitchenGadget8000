## Ingredients
- 250 g masoor dal
- 1/2 tsp panch phoron
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 dried red chili
- 1 tomato, chopped
- 1 tsp ginger paste
- 2 tbsp mustard oil
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Boil masoor dal with bay leaf until soft.
2. Heat mustard oil until smoking lightly; add panch phoron and chili.
3. Add ginger and tomato; cook 5 minutes.
4. Mix into dal and simmer 8 minutes.
5. Garnish with cilantro.

## Notes
Fragrant Bengali red lentil dal.
