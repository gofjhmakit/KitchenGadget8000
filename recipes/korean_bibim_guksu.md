## Ingredients
- 200 g somen noodles
- 2 tbsp gochujang
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp sugar
- Cucumber, julienned
- Kimchi
- Soft-boiled egg
- Sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Cook somen; rinse cold.
2. Whisk gochujang, vinegar, sesame oil, and sugar.
3. Toss noodles with spicy sauce.
4. Top with cucumber, kimchi, and egg.
5. Sprinkle sesame seeds.

## Notes
Spicy mixed Korean cold noodles.
