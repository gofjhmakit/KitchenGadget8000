## Ingredients
- 400 g beef strips
- 1 broccoli
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 garlic clove
- 15 g ginger
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp honey

## Instructions
1. Sear beef quickly in sesame oil.
2. Add garlic, ginger, and broccoli with a splash of water.
3. Stir in soy sauce and honey and cook until glossy.
4. Serve immediately.

## Notes
Best with steamed rice.
