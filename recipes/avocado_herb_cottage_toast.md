## Ingredients
- 2 slices sourdough
- 1 avocado
- 100 g cottage cheese
- 1 tbsp dill and chives
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- chili flakes

## Instructions
1. Toast the sourdough.
2. Mix cottage cheese with herbs, lemon, salt, and pepper.
3. Spread avocado on toast, top with cottage cheese, and finish with chili flakes.

## Notes
Add smoked salmon for brunch.
