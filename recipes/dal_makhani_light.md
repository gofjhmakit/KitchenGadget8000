## Ingredients
- 150 g whole black urad dal
- 100 g red kidney beans
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 200 g crushed tomatoes
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1/2 tsp kasuri methi
- 2 tbsp butter
- 400 ml water
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Soak urad dal and kidney beans overnight; simmer until very soft, about 40 minutes.
2. Sauté onion in butter until golden.
3. Add ginger-garlic paste and garam masala; cook 2 minutes.
4. Add tomatoes and simmer 10 minutes.
5. Stir in dal, mash lightly, simmer 15 minutes, finish with kasuri methi.

## Notes
Creamy Punjabi dal with less cream.
