## Ingredients
- 1 pomelo, segmented
- 100 g poached shrimp
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp palm sugar
- Fresh mint
- Shallots
- Grated coconut
- Roasted peanuts

## Instructions
1. Segment pomelo into bite pieces.
2. Whisk fish sauce, lime, and palm sugar.
3. Toss pomelo with shrimp and shallots.
4. Add dressing and mint.
5. Top with coconut and peanuts.

## Notes
Bright citrus Thai salad.
