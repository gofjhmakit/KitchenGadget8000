## Ingredients
- 300 g high-gluten flour
- 160 ml water
- 4 tbsp chili oil
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp black vinegar
- Garlic, minced
- Bok choy, blanched
- Sliced scallions

## Instructions
1. Knead dough; rest 30 minutes.
2. Roll and slap into wide belt noodles.
3. Boil noodles until chewy.
4. Whisk chili oil, soy, vinegar, and garlic.
5. Toss noodles with sauce, bok choy, and scallions.

## Notes
Hand-pulled Shaanxi belt noodles.
