## Ingredients
- 1 tsp yuzu kosho paste
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp honey

## Instructions
1. Whisk yuzu kosho with vinegar and soy.
2. Stream in olive oil.
3. Sweeten lightly with honey.
4. Use sparingly; very aromatic.
5. Excellent on seafood salads.

## Notes
Citrus-chili Japanese dressing.
