## Ingredients
- 120 g buckwheat groats
- 350 ml milk or oat drink
- 1 apple, diced
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- 1 tsp honey

## Instructions
1. Simmer buckwheat with milk until creamy.
2. Cook apple with cinnamon in a small pan until soft.
3. Serve porridge with warm apples and almond butter.

## Notes
A nutty alternative to oats.
