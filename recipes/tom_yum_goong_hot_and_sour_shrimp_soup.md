## Ingredients
- 500 g shrimp
- 1 L chicken broth
- 3 stalks lemongrass, bruised
- 4 kaffir lime leaves
- 3 galangal slices
- 200 g mushrooms
- 3 tbsp lime juice
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- Bird's eye chilies

## Instructions
1. Simmer broth with lemongrass, lime leaves, and galangal 10 minutes.
2. Add mushrooms and shrimp; cook 3 minutes.
3. Season with fish sauce and lime juice.
4. Adjust sour-spicy balance.
5. Serve immediately.

## Notes
Classic Thai hot and sour soup.
