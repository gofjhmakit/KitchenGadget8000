## Ingredients
- 400 g ground chicken
- 3 tbsp lime juice
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp toasted rice powder
- Shallots, sliced
- Fresh mint and cilantro
- Lettuce cups
- Bird's eye chilies

## Instructions
1. Cook ground chicken in dry pan until done.
2. Toss with lime, fish sauce, shallots, and chilies.
3. Add herbs and rice powder.
4. Serve in lettuce cups.
5. Eat immediately while warm.

## Notes
Bright Isaan minced chicken salad.
