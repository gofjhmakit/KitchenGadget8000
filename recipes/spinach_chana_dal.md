## Ingredients
- 200 g chana dal
- 250 g spinach, chopped
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp ginger paste
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- 2 tbsp oil
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Cook chana dal until tender.
2. Sauté cumin and onion in oil; add ginger.
3. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
4. Stir in dal and garam masala.
5. Simmer 10 minutes and season.

## Notes
Hearty spinach and split chickpea dal.
