## Ingredients
- 2 ripe mangoes, sliced
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- Fresh mint and Thai basil
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp fish sauce or soy sauce
- 1 tsp palm sugar
- Crushed peanuts

## Instructions
1. Slice mangoes and cucumber.
2. Whisk lime, fish sauce, and palm sugar.
3. Arrange fruit and herbs on plate.
4. Drizzle dressing.
5. Top with crushed peanuts.

## Notes
Sweet-savory Thai mango salad.
