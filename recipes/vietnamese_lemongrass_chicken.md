## Ingredients
- 500 g chicken thighs
- 3 stalks lemongrass, minced
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 3 garlic cloves
- Bird's eye chili
- Jasmine rice
- Pickled vegetables

## Instructions
1. Marinate chicken with lemongrass, fish sauce, sugar, and garlic 30 minutes.
2. Grill or pan-sear until charred and cooked.
3. Rest and slice.
4. Serve over rice with pickles.
5. Garnish with chili.

## Notes
Charred lemongrass chicken.
