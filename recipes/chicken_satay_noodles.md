## Ingredients
- 300 g rice noodles
- 300 g chicken thigh, cubed
- 3 tbsp peanut butter
- 1 tbsp kecap manis
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- Cucumber slices
- Fried shallots
- 2 tbsp coconut oil

## Instructions
1. Marinate chicken with turmeric and coriander; grill on skewers.
2. Whisk peanut butter with kecap manis and warm water.
3. Soak rice noodles; drain.
4. Serve noodles with satay chicken and peanut sauce.
5. Garnish with cucumber and fried shallots.

## Notes
Indonesian satay over rice noodles.
