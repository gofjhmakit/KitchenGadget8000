## Ingredients
- 400 g fresh wheat noodles
- 200 g pork belly, diced
- 1 zucchini, diced
- 1 potato, diced
- 4 tbsp chunjang (black bean paste)
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp cornstarch slurry
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Dice vegetables and blanch potato briefly.
2. Fry pork in oil; add chunjang and sugar.
3. Add vegetables and 300 ml water; simmer 15 minutes.
4. Thicken with cornstarch slurry.
5. Serve sauce over boiled noodles.

## Notes
Korean-Chinese black bean noodles.
