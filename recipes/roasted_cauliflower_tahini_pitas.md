## Ingredients
- 1 cauliflower, cut into florets
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 4 wholegrain pitas
- 150 g yogurt
- 1 tbsp tahini
- 1 cucumber

## Instructions
1. Roast cauliflower with oil, cumin, salt, and pepper at 220°C until browned.
2. Mix yogurt and tahini.
3. Fill warm pitas with cauliflower, sliced cucumber, and sauce.
4. Finish with herbs.

## Notes
Add pickled onions for tang.
