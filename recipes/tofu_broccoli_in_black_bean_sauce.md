## Ingredients
- 300 g firm tofu, cubed
- 300 g broccoli florets
- 2 tbsp fermented black bean sauce
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp cornstarch slurry
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Pan-fry tofu until golden.
2. Blanch broccoli 2 minutes.
3. Stir-fry garlic and black bean sauce.
4. Add tofu, broccoli, and soy with splash of water.
5. Thicken with cornstarch slurry.

## Notes
Savory Cantonese tofu stir fry.
