## Ingredients
- 400 g udon noodles
- 150 g kimchi, chopped
- 2 tbsp kimchi juice
- 1 tbsp gochujang
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- Sliced scallions
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Blanch udon; drain.
2. Stir-fry kimchi in oil 2 minutes.
3. Add udon, kimchi juice, gochujang, and soy sauce.
4. Toss over high heat 3 minutes.
5. Finish with sesame oil and scallions.

## Notes
Tangy spicy kimchi udon.
