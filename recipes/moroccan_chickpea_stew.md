## Ingredients
- 400 g canned chickpeas
- 400 g canned tomatoes
- 1 onion
- 2 carrots, diced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp paprika
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion and carrots in oil.
2. Add spices and stir 30 seconds.
3. Add tomatoes, chickpeas, and 250 ml water.
4. Simmer 20 minutes; finish with cilantro.

## Notes
Serve with couscous or bread.
