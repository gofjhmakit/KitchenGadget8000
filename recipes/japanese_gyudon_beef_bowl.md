## Ingredients
- 250 g thinly sliced beef
- 1 onion, sliced
- 3 tbsp dashi
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 bowls cooked rice
- Pickled ginger
- Onsen egg

## Instructions
1. Simmer onion in dashi, soy, mirin, and sugar.
2. Add beef; cook 3 minutes.
3. Serve over rice.
4. Top with onsen egg and pickled ginger.
5. Drizzle remaining sauce.

## Notes
Fast Japanese beef rice bowl.
