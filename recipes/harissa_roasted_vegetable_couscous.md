## Ingredients
- 1 courgette
- 1 aubergine
- 1 red pepper
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp harissa
- 220 g wholewheat couscous
- 30 g mint

## Instructions
1. Toss chopped vegetables with olive oil and harissa.
2. Roast at 220°C until tender and charred.
3. Prepare couscous with hot water and fluff.
4. Fold through vegetables and mint.

## Notes
Add chickpeas for extra protein.
