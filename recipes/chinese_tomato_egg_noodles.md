## Ingredients
- 300 g fresh wheat noodles
- 3 tomatoes, chopped
- 3 eggs, beaten
- 2 tbsp ketchup
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Sliced scallions

## Instructions
1. Scramble eggs in oil; set aside.
2. Cook tomatoes and garlic until saucy.
3. Add ketchup, sugar, and splash of water.
4. Toss cooked noodles in tomato sauce.
5. Fold in eggs and scallions.

## Notes
Home-style tomato egg noodles.
