## Ingredients
- 400 g fish fillet, cubed
- 1 L water
- 2 tbsp tamarind paste
- 2 tomatoes, wedges
- Pineapple chunks
- Bean sprouts
- Okra
- Rice paddy herb
- Fish sauce

## Instructions
1. Simmer tamarind and tomatoes 10 minutes.
2. Add pineapple and okra.
3. Add fish; cook 5 minutes.
4. Season with fish sauce.
5. Finish with sprouts and herbs off heat.

## Notes
Sweet-sour Mekong Delta soup.
