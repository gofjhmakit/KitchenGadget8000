## Ingredients
- 400 g cod fillets
- 400 g cherry tomatoes
- 2 garlic cloves, sliced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp capers
- Fresh parsley
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Place cod in a baking dish with tomatoes and garlic.
2. Drizzle with olive oil; season.
3. Bake at 200°C for 18 minutes until fish flakes.
4. Scatter capers and parsley before serving.

## Notes
Mediterranean one-dish dinner.
