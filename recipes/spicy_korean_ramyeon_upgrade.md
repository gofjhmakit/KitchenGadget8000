## Ingredients
- 1 pack Korean ramyeon noodles
- 1 egg
- 50 g sliced cheese
- 2 tbsp kimchi
- 1 tbsp gochugaru
- Sliced scallions
- Sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Boil noodles with spice packet 4 minutes.
2. Crack egg into broth; poach 1 minute.
3. Add kimchi and gochugaru.
4. Top with cheese off heat.
5. Garnish with scallions and sesame seeds.

## Notes
Elevated instant ramyeon bowl.
