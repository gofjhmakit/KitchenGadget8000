## Ingredients
- 400 g chicken breast, cubed
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp black vinegar
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Dried chilies
- Roasted peanuts
- Sichuan peppercorns
- Scallions
- Ginger and garlic

## Instructions
1. Stir-fry chicken until cooked; set aside.
2. Fry chilies, peppercorns, ginger, and garlic.
3. Return chicken with soy, vinegar, and sugar.
4. Toss in peanuts and scallions.
5. Serve immediately.

## Notes
Classic Sichuan stir fry.
