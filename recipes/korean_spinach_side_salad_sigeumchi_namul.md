## Ingredients
- 400 g spinach
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp toasted sesame seeds
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions
1. Blanch spinach 30 seconds; plunge in ice water.
2. Squeeze spinach dry thoroughly.
3. Mix garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, and salt.
4. Toss spinach with seasoning.
5. Sprinkle sesame seeds.

## Notes
Classic Korean seasoned spinach.
