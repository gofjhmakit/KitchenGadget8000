## Ingredients
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 100 g daikon, sliced
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- Kombu strip

## Instructions
1. Salt vegetables 10 minutes; squeeze.
2. Toss with vinegar, sugar, and kombu.
3. Rest 20 minutes.
4. Serve chilled.
5. Light palate-cleansing pickles.

## Notes
Everyday Japanese quick pickles.
