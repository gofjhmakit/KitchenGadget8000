## Ingredients
- 400 g egg noodles
- 300 g chicken breast, sliced
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 150 g bean sprouts
- 3 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Sliced scallions

## Instructions
1. Boil egg noodles 3 minutes; rinse and drain.
2. Stir-fry chicken in oil until cooked; set aside.
3. Stir-fry carrot and garlic 2 minutes.
4. Return chicken with noodles, oyster sauce, and soy sauce.
5. Toss in bean sprouts and scallions; serve immediately.

## Notes
Cantonese-style wok noodles.
