## Ingredients
- 800 ml dashi
- 3 tbsp white miso
- 200 g silken tofu, cubed
- 2 tbsp dried wakame
- 2 scallions, sliced

## Instructions
1. Heat dashi to just below boiling.
2. Rehydrate wakame in warm water.
3. Dissolve miso in ladle of dashi; stir into pot.
4. Add tofu and wakame; heat gently.
5. Serve with scallions.

## Notes
Everyday Japanese miso soup.
