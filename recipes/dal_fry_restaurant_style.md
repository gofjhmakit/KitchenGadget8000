## Ingredients
- 150 g chana dal
- 100 g masoor dal
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 tomatoes, pureed
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp kasuri methi
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp garam masala
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Cook both dals until smooth.
2. Sauté onion in butter until browned.
3. Add ginger-garlic and tomato puree; cook 10 minutes.
4. Add garam masala and kasuri methi.
5. Simmer with dal 12 minutes until thick.

## Notes
Thick dhaba-style dal fry.
