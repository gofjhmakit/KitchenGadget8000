## Ingredients
- 2 Chinese eggplants, sliced
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp black vinegar
- 1 tbsp chili oil
- Scallions
- Cilantro

## Instructions
1. Steam eggplant slices 10 minutes until tender.
2. Mix soy sauce, vinegar, and chili oil.
3. Top eggplant with raw minced garlic.
4. Pour hot sauce over to mellow garlic.
5. Garnish with scallions and cilantro.

## Notes
Silky steamed eggplant side.
