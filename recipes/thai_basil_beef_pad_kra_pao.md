## Ingredients
- 300 g ground beef
- 3 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- Thai basil leaves
- Bird's eye chilies
- Garlic
- Fried egg
- Jasmine rice

## Instructions
1. Stir-fry garlic and chilies.
2. Add beef; cook until browned.
3. Season with oyster sauce, soy, and fish sauce.
4. Fold in Thai basil off heat.
5. Serve over rice with fried egg.

## Notes
Spicy Thai basil minced beef.
