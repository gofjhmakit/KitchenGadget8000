## Ingredients
- 250 g chana dal (split chickpeas)
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 tomatoes, pureed
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- 2 tbsp oil
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Pressure-cook or simmer chana dal with salt until tender but holding shape.
2. Heat oil; add cumin and onion; cook until browned.
3. Add ginger-garlic paste and chili powder; cook 1 minute.
4. Stir in tomato puree and simmer 8 minutes.
5. Mix dal into masala, finish with garam masala and cilantro.

## Notes
Restaurant-style thick chana dal.
