## Ingredients
- 120 g bulgur wheat
- Large bunch parsley, finely chopped
- Small bunch mint, chopped
- 4 tomatoes, diced
- 4 spring onions, sliced
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Soak bulgur in boiling water 20 minutes; drain well.
2. Combine parsley, mint, tomatoes, and spring onions.
3. Dress with lemon, olive oil, and salt.
4. Fold in bulgur and chill before serving.

## Notes
Fresh Lebanese herb salad.
