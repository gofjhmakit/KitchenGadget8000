## Ingredients
- 250 g brown lentils
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 400 g canned tomatoes
- 1.5 L vegetable broth
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 2 bay leaves

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion, carrot, and celery in oil for 8 minutes.
2. Add lentils, tomatoes, broth, thyme, and bay leaves.
3. Simmer 30 minutes until lentils are tender.
4. Season and serve with crusty bread.

## Notes
Hearty budget soup.
