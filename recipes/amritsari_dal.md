## Ingredients
- 150 g chana dal
- 100 g moong dal
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 2 tbsp butter
- Fresh ginger julienne
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Cook both dals together until smooth.
2. Sauté onion in butter until deep golden.
3. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes; cook 8 minutes.
4. Stir in garam masala and dal.
5. Simmer 10 minutes; top with fresh ginger.

## Notes
Buttery Punjabi mixed dal.
