## Ingredients
- 300 g basmati rice
- 1 tbsp cumin seeds
- 2 tbsp ghee or oil
- 500 ml water
- 2 bay leaves
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Rinse rice and soak 15 minutes.
2. Toast cumin in ghee until fragrant.
3. Add rice, bay leaves, water, and salt.
4. Simmer covered 15 minutes.
5. Rest 5 minutes and fluff.

## Notes
Cumin-scented basmati rice.
