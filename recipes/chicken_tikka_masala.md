## Ingredients
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
- 2 tbsp garam masala
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- Salt to taste
- Fresh cilantro for garnish
- 2.5 lbs, boneless skinless chicken thighs

## Instructions
1. Place chicken thighs in the slow cooker.
2. In a bowl, combine diced tomatoes, tomato paste, onion, garlic, ginger, garam masala, cumin, turmeric, paprika, cayenne, and salt. Pour over chicken.
3. Cover and cook on LOW for 6 hours or HIGH for 3 hours.
4. Remove chicken and shred or cube it, then return to the sauce.
5. Stir in heavy cream and let it warm through for 10-15 minutes on HIGH.
6. Cook jasmine rice according to package directions.
7. Serve tikka masala over rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.
