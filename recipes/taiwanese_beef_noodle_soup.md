## Ingredients
- 500 g beef chuck, cubed
- 400 g wheat noodles
- 2 tbsp doubanjiang
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- Star anise
- Soy sauce
- Bok choy
- Pickled mustard greens

## Instructions
1. Braise beef with doubanjiang, tomato, and spices 90 minutes.
2. Cook noodles separately.
3. Blanch bok choy.
4. Ladle braising liquid and beef over noodles.
5. Serve with pickled mustard greens.

## Notes
Rich red-braised Taiwanese classic.
