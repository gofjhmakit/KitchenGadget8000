## Ingredients
- 400 g frozen udon
- 600 ml mentsuyu diluted with water
- Sliced scallions
- Shichimi togarashi
- Grated daikon

## Instructions
1. Heat mentsuyu broth.
2. Blanch udon in boiling water 2 minutes.
3. Place udon in bowls.
4. Ladle hot broth over.
5. Garnish with scallions, shichimi, and daikon.

## Notes
Minimal clear udon soup.
